THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Elizabeth Wanjiru Njomo (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.1 hectare or thereabouts, known as Ngong/Ngong/5318, situate in the district of Kajiado North, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Kiambu in succession cause No. 64 of 2012, has issued grant of letters of administration to John Francis Njomo, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said Elizabeth Wanjiru Njomo (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Elizabeth Wanjiru Njomo (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 20th November, 2015.
