THE CIVIL PROCEDURE ACT
(CAP.  21)
ESTABLISHMENT
TAKE NOTICE that the Chief Justice/President, Supreme Court of Kenya has established Mpeketoni Law Courts, a magistrate’s court that is within supervisory jurisdiction of Garsen High Court, with effect from 6th June, 2016.
Dated the 5th May, 2016.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice and President, Supreme Court of Kenya.
