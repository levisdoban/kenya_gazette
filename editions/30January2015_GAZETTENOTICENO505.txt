THE WATER ACT
(Cap. 372)
Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 46 (3) (a) of the Water Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, appoint—
Antony Meitiaki Kiroken
to be Chairman of the Water Services Regulatory Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 2nd February, 2015.
Dated the 28th January, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
