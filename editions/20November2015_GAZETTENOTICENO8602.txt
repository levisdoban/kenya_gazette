THE HOUSING ACT
(Cap. 117)
DESIGNATION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (2) of the Housing Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Land, Housing and Urban Development designates—
Francis Gitau Mungai
as the Chairman of the National Housing Corporation, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 13th November, 2015.
Dated the 13th November, 2015.
FRED MATIANG’I,
                                                                Ag. Cabinet Secretary,
Ministry of Land, Housing and Urban Development.
