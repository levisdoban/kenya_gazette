THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA THE JUDICIAL SERVICE COMMISSION (NO. 1 OF 2011)
APPOINTMENT OF JUDGES OF THE HIGH COURT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Article 166 (1) (h) of the Constitution, as read with Article 172 (1) (a) of the Constitution and section 30 of the First Schedule of the Judicial Service Act, 2011, I Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, in accordance with the recommendation of the Judicial Service Commission, appoint —
Bwonwong'a Justus Momanyi, Onguto Joseph Louis Omondi, Aburili Roselyne Ekirapa, Enock Chacha Mwita,
Robert Kipkoech Limo,
Mutungi Charles Kariuki,
Mrima Antony Charo,
Janet Nzilani Mulwa,
Nagillah Chrispin Beda,
Farah Amin Shaikh Mohamed, Margaret Waringa Muigai,
to be judges of the High Court of the Republic of Kenya, with effect from 1st July, 2014.
Dated the 27th June, 2014.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
