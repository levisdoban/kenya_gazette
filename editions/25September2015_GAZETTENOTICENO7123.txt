REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Muthee Njuguna (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 5.4 acres or thereabout, known as Limuru/Ngecha/402, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the senior principal magistrate’s court at Limuru in succession cause No. 149 of 2014, has issued grant of letters of administration to Kamau Kangethe, and whereas the said title deed issued to Muthee Njuguna (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Muthee Njuguna (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
