THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Gerald Mutethia Muguna (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Ntima/Igoki/4830, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court in succession cause No. 685 of 2013, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of (1) Kendi M’Rintari and (2) Penina Gaceri, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the land title deed in respect of Gerald Mutethia Muguna (deceased), is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in the names of (1) Kendi M’Rintari and (2) Penina Gaceri, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Gerald Mutethia Muguna (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 30th October, 2014.
B. K. KAMWARO,
MR/8477030                                   Land Registrar, Meru District.
