THE RESTRICTIVE TRADE PRACTICES, MONOPOLIES AND PRICE CONTROL ACT
(CAP. 504)
PROPOSED PURCHASE OF THE ENTIRE ISSUED SHARE
CAPITAL OF ASSIA ANIMAL HEALTH LIMITED
 BY BIMEDA ANIMAL HEALTH (KENYA) LIMITED
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 31 of the Restrictive Trade Practices, Monopolies and Price Control Act, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Finance authorizes the proposed purchase of the entire issued share capital of Assia Animal Health Limited by Bimeda Animal Health (Kenya) Limited.
 Dated the 6th April, 2011.
UHURU KENYATTA,
Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Finance.
