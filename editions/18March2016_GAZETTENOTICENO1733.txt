
				Page: 999GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 1733
THE TOURISM ACT
(No. 28 of 2011)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 32 (1) (a) of the Tourism Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Jimmy Kariuki
to be the Chairperson of the Kenya Tourism Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from  the 18th March, 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
