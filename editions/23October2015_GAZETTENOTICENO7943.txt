REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Maina Johana Ngure (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.046 hectare or thereabouts, known as Lari/Magina/T. 287, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the senior resident magistrate’s court at Engineer in succession cause No. 43 of 2014, has issued grant of letters of administration to Tabitha Wanjiku Maina, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said Maina Johana Ngure (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Maina Johana Ngure (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
