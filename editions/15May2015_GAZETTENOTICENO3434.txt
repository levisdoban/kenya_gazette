THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 83 (4) of the Water Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Environment, Water and Natural Resources appoints—
Principal Secretary, the National Treasury,
Principal Secretary, State Department of Water and Regional Authorities,
to be Trustees of  the Water Services Trust Fund, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 17th April, 2015.
Dated the 16th May, 2015.
JUDI W. WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary,
Environment, Water and Natural Resources.
