(CAP. 265)
THE TOWN COUNCIL OF UKWALA
APPOINTMENT OF PROSECUTOR
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 260 (1) of the Local Government Act, the Town Council of Ukwala has under its Full Council Minute No. UTC/FC/4/201 held on 15th March, 2011 appointed—
Stephen Otieno Nyagilo
 to be a public prosecutor for all their cases under the Local Government Act and any other related Act before the subordinate courts of Ukwala, Siaya, Maseno, Bondo, Kisumu and Busia.
Dated the 31st March, 2011.
