THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(CAP. 490)
INQUIRY ORDER
WHEREAS I have on my own accord, decided that an inquiry be held into the—
(a) by-laws;
(b) working and financial conditions;
(c)   the conduct of the management committee, and past or present members or officers;
of Kayatta Farmers Co-operative Society Limited (CS/7746)  and in accordance with section 58 and as read with section 73 of the Co-operative Societies Act of the laws of Kenya.
Now therefore I authorize (1) Pauline Nyambura Kinuthia, Co-operative Officer, Gatundu and (2) Boniface M. Karanja, Senior Co-operative Auditor, Nairobi Province, to hold an inquiry within sixteen (16) days from the date thereof at such place and time as may be expedient and duly notified by them.
The attention of the officers and members of the society is directed to the following sections of the Co-operative Societies Act.
Section 60 (1)—Cost of inquiry.
Section 60 (2)—Recovery of costs of expenses.
Section 73—Surcharges.
Section 94—Offences
Dated the 24th January, 2010.
F. F. ODHIAMBO,
MR1680396            Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
