PROBATE AND ADMINISTRATION
TAKE NOTICE that after thirty (30) days from the date of this Gazette, and unless cause be shown to the contrary, I intend to apply to the High Court at Nairobi for representation of the estates of the persons named in the second column of the Schedule hereto, who died on the date respectively set forth against their names.
And further take notice that all persons having any claims against or interest in the estate of the said deceased persons are required to prove such claims or interests before me within two (2) months from the date of this Gazette, after which the date claims and interests so proved will be paid and satisfied and the several estates distributed according to law.
