THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 33)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Obadiah Ndwiga Muchiri, of Nembure, Embu in the Republic of Kenya, is registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Gaturi/Nembure/8874, containing 0.533 hectare or thereabouts, situate in the district of Embu, and whereas C.M.C.C. at Embu, in civil suit No. 344 of 1999, has ordered that the said piece of land be registered in the name of Cheleste Mbogo Njeru, and whereas the executive officer of the said court has in pursuance to an order of the said court executed a transfer of the said piece of land in favour of Cheleste Mbogo Njeru, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land to the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of transfer and issue a land title deed to the said Cheleste Mbogo Njeru, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Obadiah Ndwiga Muchiri, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 22nd July, 2011.
J. M. MUNGUTI,
MR/7394529                                  Land Registrar, Embu District.
