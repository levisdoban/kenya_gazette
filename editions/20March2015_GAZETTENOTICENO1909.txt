THE LAND ACT (NO. 6 OF 2012)
MWEA IRRIGATION EXPANSION
Inquiry
IN PURSUANCE of the transitional provisions contained in section 162 (2) of the Land Act, 2012, and section 9 (1) of the Land Acquisition Act (Cap. 295 repealed). The National Land Commission gives notice that inquiries to the hearing of claims to compensation for interested parties in the land required for Mwea Irrigation Expansion shall be held on Tuesday, 14th April, 2015, at the Deputy Commissioner’s office, Kirinyaga East.
