THE TRANSFER OF BUSINESSES ACT
(Cap. 500)
ALPHA DAIRY PRODUCTS LIMITED AND RAZCO LIMITED
BUSINESS TRANSFER
NOTICE is given under the Transfer of Businesses Act (Cap. 500) of the laws of Kenya, that the ice cream production and trading business carried on by Alpha Dairy Products Limited at Road “A”, off Enterprise Road, P.O. Box 10357–00100, Nairobu, Kenya, will be transferred on or about 30th June, 2015, (subject to fulfilment of certain conditions precedent)  to Razco Limited which  will carry on the business under the name Razco Limited at the same premises.
The address of transferor is P.O. Box 10357–00100, Nairobi, Kenya.
The address of the transferee is P.O. Box 63538–00619, Nairobi, Kenya.
All money debts or liabilities due and owing by the Transferor in respect of the business up to the date of transfer as set out above shall be received and paid by the transferor. The transferee is not assuming nor is it intended to assume any liabilities (save for certain obligations to customers in respect of customer orders) incurred by the transferor in the business up to the date of transfer.
Dated the 3rd June, 2015.
