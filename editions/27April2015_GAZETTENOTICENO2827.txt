THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT

APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) (a) of the State Corporations Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces of the Republic of Kenya, appoint—
Gershom Otachi
to be the Chairperson of Geothermal Development Company,  for a period of three (3) years with effect from the 20th May, 2015.
Dated the 17th April, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
