THE CENTRAL BANK OF KENYA ACT
(Cap. 49)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME
IT IS NOTIFIED for general information that the specified bank formerly known as K-Rep Bank Limited has by special resolution of the Board and the approval of the Registrar of Companies, effected a
change of name from K-Rep Bank Limited to Sidian Bank Limited, with effect from the 4th April, 2016.
Dated the 1st April, 2016.
PATRICK NJOROGE,
Governor, Central Bank of Kenya.
