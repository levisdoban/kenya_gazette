THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENTS
WHEREAS (1) Simion Muchiri Macharia and (2) Peter Mwangi Muchiri, are the registered proprietors of those pieces of land known as Mau Summit/Molo Block 5/1347 and 1348, containing 1.06 and 1.06 hectares or thereabout, respectively, situate in the district of Nakuru, and whereas in the senior principal magistrate’s court at Molo Land Dispute No. 40 of 2008 has ordered that the said piece of land be reverted back to the original number Mau Summit/Molo Block 5/138 to Muchiri Gachigi, and whereas the court has directed the execution of the said transfers of parcels in favour of Muchiri Gachigi, and whereas all efforts made to compel (1) Simon Muchiri Macharia and (2) Peter Mwangi Muchiri to surrender the said titles in respect of the said piece of land, notice is hereby given that after expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deeds and proceed with the registration of the said court order and issued land title deeds to the said Muchiri Gachigi, and upon the registration the land title deeds issued earlier to the said (1) Simon Muchiri Macharia and (2) Peter Mwangi Muchiri, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 26th February, 2016.
C. W. SUNGUTI,
MR/8767443                               Land Registrar, Nakuru District.
