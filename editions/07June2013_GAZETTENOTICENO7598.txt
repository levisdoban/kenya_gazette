THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 33)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Daniel Itumo Wambua, is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Athi River/Athi River Block 1/579, situate in Machakos District, and whereas the High Court at Machakos in succession cause No. 414 of 2003 has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Jacinta Mwikali Maingi, of P.O. Box 640–90100 Machakos, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the land certificate issued in respect of the said piece of land to the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received, I intend to dispense with production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of transfer and issue a land certificate to Jacinta Mwikali Wambua, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Daniel Itumo Wambua, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 7th June, 2013.
