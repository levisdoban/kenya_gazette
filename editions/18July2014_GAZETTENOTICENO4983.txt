THE PHYSICAL PLANNING ACT

COMPLETION OF ADVISORY DEVELOPMENT PLAN
(PDP No. R16/2014/01 for Advisory Development)
NOTICE is given that the above-mentioned development plan was on 25th June, 2014, completed.
The Advisory development plan relates to land situated within Kapkures Trading Centre.
A copy of the part development plan has been deposited for public inspection at the office of the Department of Lands, Housing and Urban Development in Bomet and County Physical Planning Office, Bomet.
The copy so deposited is available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the offices of the Department of Lands, Housing and Urban Development in Bomet and County Physical Planning Office, Bomet, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objections to the above-named part development plan may send such representations or objections in writing to be received by the County Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 19–20400, Bomet, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
Dated the 25th June, 2014.
