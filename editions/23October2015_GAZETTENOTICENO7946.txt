THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Robinson Karuri Mugweru, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nanyuki/Marura Block III/2869 (Sweetwaters), situate in the district of Laikipia, and whereas the High Court in succession cause No. 620 of 2013, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of (1) Simon Kariuki Kioni and (2) Charles Mwangi Mugweru, and whereas the said administrators have executed an application to be registered as proprietors by transmission of R. L. 19, and Whereas The land title deed of the said piece of land is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission of R. L. 19 in the names of (1) Simon Kariuki Kioni and (2) Charles Mwangi Mugweru, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Robinson Karuri Mugweru (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 23rd October, 2015.
P. M. MUTEGI,
MR/8220978                              Land Registrar, Laikipia District.
