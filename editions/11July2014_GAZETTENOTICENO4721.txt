THE PHYSICAL PLANNING ACT
(CAP. 286)
COMPLETION OF PART DEVELOPMENT PLAN
(PDP No. R1268/2014/01 for Existing Site for Mulot Police Station)
NOTICE is given that the above-mentioned development plan was on 17th June, 2014, completed.
The development plan relates to land situated within Narok South Sub-County.
A copy of the part development plan has been deposited for public inspection at the office of the County Physical Planning Officer, at former Town Council Office, Narok Town along Narok–Nakuru Road, and the Sub-County Commissioner’s Offices, Narok South Sub-County.
The copy so deposited is available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the offices of the County Physical Planning Officer, at former Town Council Office, Narok Town along Narok–Nakuru Road, and the Sub-County Commissioner’s Offices, Narok South Sub-County, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objections to the above-named part development plan may send such representations or objections in writing to be received by the County Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 753, Narok, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
Dated the 26th June, 2014.
