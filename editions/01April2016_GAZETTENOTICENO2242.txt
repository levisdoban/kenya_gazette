THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(Cap. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
(Extension Order)
WHEREAS by an extension order dated 10th October, 2014, I authorize Stephen W. Kamau, Senior  Co-operative Officer, Headquarters, to be liquidator of Boulevard Hotel Sacco Society Limited (CS/3585) (in liquidation), for a period not exceeding one year, and whereas the said Stephen W. Kamau, Senior Co-operative Officer, Headquarters, has not been able to complete the liquidation.
Now therefore, I extend the period of liquidation with effect from 10th October, 2015, for another period not exceeding one year for the said Stephen W. Kamau, Senior Co-operative Officer, Headquarters, to act as liquidator in the matter of the said co-operative society.
Dated the 10th October, 2015.
P. N. GICHUKI,
MR/8858115     Ag. Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
