DECLARATION OF PERSONS ELECTED TO LELMOKWO/NGECHEK WARD—NANDI COUNTY ASSEMBLY, WEST UYOMA WARD—SIAYA COUNTY ASSEMBLY AND NGOBIT WARD—LAIKIPIA COUNTY ASSEMBLY, DURING THE BY–ELECTION HELD ON 3RD JUNE, 2016
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 88 (4), 193 and 194 of the Constitution of Kenya, section 4 of the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission Act, 2011, sections 19, 20, 22, 25, 26, 38 and 39 of the Elections Act, 2011 and regulations 83 and 87 (1) (b), 4 (b), of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission declares that the persons listed in Column 1 and 2 of the Schedule hereunder were elected as County Assembly Ward Representatives for Lelmokwo/Ngechek, West Uyoma and Ngobit Wards in Nandi, Siaya and Laikipia County Assemblies, respectively, having received the majority of the votes cast in the by- elections held on 3rd June, 2016 and complied with the provisions of the Constitution, Elections Act, 2011 and Elections (General) Regulations (2012).
