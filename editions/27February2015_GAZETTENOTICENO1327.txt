THE RECORDS DISPOSAL (COURTS) RULES
(Cap. 14, Sub. Leg.)
IN THE SENIOR RESIDENT MAGISTRATE’S COURT AT WINAM
INTENDED DESTRUCTION OF COURT RECORDS
IN ACCORDANCE with the Records Disposal (Courts) Rules, notice is given that three (3) months after the date of publication of this notice, the Senior Resident Magistrate’s Court at Winam, intends to apply to the Chief Justice/President of the Supreme Court, for leave to destroy the records, books and papers of the Senior Resident Magistrate’s Court at Winam, as set out below:
Criminal cases                                    2000–2009
A comprehensive list of all condemned records that qualify to be disposed under the Act can be obtained and perused at the Senior Resident Magistrate’s Court Registry, Winam.
Any person desiring the return of any exhibit in any of the above cases must make his/her claim within the time stipulated in this publication should do so before the expiry of the notice.
All exhibits to which no claim is substantiated before the destruction of the records shall under section 4 be deemed to be part of the records for the purposes of destruction.
Dated the 9th February, 2015.
BERNARD KASAVULI,
Senior Resident Magistrate, Winam.
