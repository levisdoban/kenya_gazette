THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
Notification of Special Sittings of the
National Assembly
PURSUANT to standing order 29 (3) of the Standing Orders of the National Assembly, it is notified for the information of Members of the National Assembly and the general public that two special sittings of the Assembly shall be held in the National Assembly Chamber in the Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi, on Thursday, 18th December, 2014 at 9.30 a.m. and at 2.30 p.m. for purposes of—
(a)  consideration of a Special Motion relating to the report of the Committee on Appointments on the approval of Hon. Joseph ole Nkaiserry to the office of Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government;
(b) consideration of a Special Motion relating to the report of the Committee on the Constituencies Development Fund on the approval of the proposed members of the Constituencies Development Fund Board;
(c)  Committee of the Whole House and Third Reading of the Security Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2014.
Dated the 15th December, 2014.
JUSTIN MUTURI,
Speaker of the National Assembly.
