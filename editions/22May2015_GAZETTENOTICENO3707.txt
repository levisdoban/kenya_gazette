THE MICROFINANCE ACT
 (No. 19 of 2006)
THE MICROFINANCE (DEPOSIT-TAKING MICROFINANCE INSTITUTIONS) REGULATIONS, 2008
 (L.N. 58 of 2008)
LICENSING OF A MICROFINANCE BANK
PURSUANT to regulation 8 (1) of the Microfinance (Deposit-Taking Microfinance Institutions) Regulations, 2008, it is notified for the general information of the public that the Central Bank of Kenya has licensed Choice Microfinance Bank Limited as a community microfinance bank to carry out microfinance banking business in Kajiado North Constituency within Kajiado County with effect from the 13th May, 2015.
Dated the 20th May, 2015.
HARON SIRIMA,
Deputy Governor, Central Bank of Kenya.
