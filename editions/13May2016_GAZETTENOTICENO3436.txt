The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the office of the County Director, Land and Physical Planning, Nakuru, at Ardhi House, 3rd Floor, Room 8 and Sub-County Administrator, Nakuru West, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m., Monday to Friday.

Box 1028, Nakuru, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
