THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 51 of the Water Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Environment, Water and Natural Resources appoints—
Director Water Services, State Department of Water and Regional Authorities,
Principal Secretary, the National Treasury,
to be Members  of  the  Rift Valley Water Services Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 17th April, 2015.
Dated the 16th May, 2015.
JUDI W. WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary,
Environment, Water and Natural Resources.
