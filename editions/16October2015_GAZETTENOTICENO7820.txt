THE PHYSICAL PLANNING ACT
(Cap. 286)
COMPLETION OF PART DEVELOPMENT PLAN.
(PDP No. LMU/520/II/1/15–proposed site for 960MW Lamu Coal Fired Power Plant)
NOTICE is given that the abovementioned development plan was on 6th October, 2015, completed.
The part development plan relates to land situated within Hindi (Kwasasi area).
Copies of the part development plans are available for public inspection at the office of the CEC-Land, Lamu (Forest House), County Physical Office (Ardhi House, Mokowe), Lamu, Ward Administrator, Mokowe.
The copies so deposited are available for public inspection at the office of the CEC-Land, Lamu (Forest House), County Physical Planning Office (Ardhi House, Mokowe), Lamu, Ward Administrator, Mokowe, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objection to the above-named part development plan may send such representation or objection in writing to the CEC, Land, Chief Officer, Land or County Physical Planning Office, P.O. Box 288–80500, Lamu, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and any such representation or objection shall state the ground on which it is made.
