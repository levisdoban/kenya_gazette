The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the offices of the District Physical Planning Officer, Lamu, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.

Box 288-80500, Lamu, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
