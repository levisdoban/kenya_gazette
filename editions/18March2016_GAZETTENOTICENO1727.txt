THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
(Cap. 446)
THE KENYA INDUSTRIAL ESTATES
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) (a) of the State Corporations Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Rehema Jaldesa
to be the Chairperson of the Kenya Industrial Estates, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 18th March 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
