THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 33)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENTS
WHEREAS M’Kararu M’Mukua (deceased), is the registered proprietor of those pieces of land known as Nyaki/Nkabune/560 and Nyaki/Giaki/259, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High court at Meru, in succession cause No. H.C/R.M 459 of 2011, has issued grant of letters of administration to Stella Kagwiria Mutuma, and whereas the land title deeds issued earlier to the said M’Kararu M’Mukua (deceased) have been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deeds and proceed with the registration of the instruments of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deeds issued earlier to the said M’Kararu M’Mukua (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 3rd May, 2013.
H. S. W. MUSUMIAH,
MR/1933092                                   Land Registrar, Meru District.
