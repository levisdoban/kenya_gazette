THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS M’Murithi Murugu (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Igoji/Kianjogu/476, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court of Kenya in succession cause No. 89 of 2004, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant to Charity Njiru, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said M’Murithi Murugu (deceased), has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 in the name of Charity Njiru, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to M’Murithi Murugu (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 24th March, 2016.
B. K. KAMWARO,
