
				Page: 671GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 1980
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
SPECIAL SITTING OF PARLIAMENT
NOTICE is given to all Senators that pursuant to Article 132 (1) (b) and (c) (i) and (iii) of the Constitution of Kenya, 2010, His Excellency, Hon. Uhuru Kenyatta, C.G.H., President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, wishes to address a Special Sitting of Parliament on Thursday, 26th March, 2015.
Accordingly, I notify all Senators that a Special Sitting of Parliament shall be held on Thursday, 26th March, 2015 commencing at 2:30 p.m. at the National Assembly Chamber, Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi.
Dated the 20th March, 2015.
EKWEE ETHURO,
Speaker of the Senate.
