THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 33)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS M’Mukiri M’Mburugu (deceased), is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Abothuguchi/Kariene/2511, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court, in succession cause No. 320 of 2006, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of Elizabeth Kirigo Mukiri, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission RL. 19, and whereas the title deed in respect of the said piece of land registered in the name of M’Mukiri M’Mburugu is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission RL. 19 in the name of Elizabeth Kirigo Mukiri, and upon such registration, the land title deed issued earlier to the said M’Mukiri M’Mburugu (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 22nd February, 2013.
B. K. KAMWARO,
MR/1485425                                   Land Registrar, Meru District.
