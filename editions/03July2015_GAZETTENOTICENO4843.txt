NOTIFICATION OF BAR EXAMINATION DATES
PURSUANT to the Statute Law (Miscellaneous Amendment) Act, 2014 amending the Legal Education Act No. 27 of 2012, it is notified for general information that the Council of Legal Education Bar Examinations shall be held at the Kenya School of Law, Lang’ata South Road, Karen, Nairobi, from 27th July, 2015 to 6th August, 2015, both dates inclusive.

The deadline for registration for the examinations is Tuesday, 30th June, 2015.
Only candidates who have paid the requisite fees and have duly registered for the examinations shall be issued with an examination card and allowed in the examination venue. Examination cards must be collected from CLE offices from 20th July, 2015 to 24th July, 2015. 
Candidates must bring the examination card and student identity card for all examinations.
