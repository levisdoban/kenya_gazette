THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Mwaniki Mathagu Mwaniki (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nanyuki/South Timau Block 2/57, situate in the district of Laikipia, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Nanyuki in succession cause No. 30 of 2004, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of Kephins C. F. Migwi Mwaniki, and whereas the said administrator has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission of R. L. 19, and whereas the land title deed issued earlier has been reported lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in the name of to Kephins C. F. Migwi Mwaniki, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Mwaniki Mathagu Mwaniki (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
