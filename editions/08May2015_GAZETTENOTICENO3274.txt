THE COMPANIES ACT
(Cap. 486)
DAIMA BANK LIMITED
(In Liquidation)
5th Payment to Depositors and Creditors
NOTICE is given that the Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation—KDIC (formerly Deposit Protection Fund Board), in exercise of the powers conferred under rule 107 of the Companies (Winding-up) Rules, will commence the 5th payment from 4th May, 2015, to all eligible depositors and creditors of Daima Bank Limited (in liquidation) who have proved their debts.
All payments shall be through Electornic Funds Trusfer (EFT). In this regard, depositors and creditors are required to provide their Bank Account details to the liquidator agent at the offices of KDIC located on the 2nd Floor, CBK Pension House (formerly Marshall House), Harambee Avenue, Nairobi, between 9.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. from Monday to Friday.
The account details should be personally delivered by the eligible payees to facilitate proper identification.
Claimants are further advised to note that this payment is valid for a period of one (1) year from the date of commencement of payment stated above.
Dated the 17th April, 2015.
AGGREY JONATHAN BETT,
MR/7407285           Ag. CEO, Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation.
