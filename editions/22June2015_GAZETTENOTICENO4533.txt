THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENT OF BOMENT
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to Standing Order 29 of the Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Bomet, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly of Bomet and the general public that the Assembly shall have a special sitting on Tuesday,  23rd of June, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. at the County Assembly Chamber in Bomet for the tabling of the report of the Committee on Budget and Appropriation on the Bomet County MTEF budget 2015/16-2017/18 and annual budget for the year ending 30th June, 2016.
Dated the 22nd June, 2015.
G. K. KIPNG’ETICH,
Speaker.
