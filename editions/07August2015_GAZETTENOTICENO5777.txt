THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(Cap. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
Extension Order
WHEREAS by liquidation order dated 4th August, 2014, I appointed (1) Peter I. Kimotho, Chief Co-operative Officer, Headquarters, (2) Suleiman Wandati, Legal Officer, Headquarters, to be liquidators of Methi and Swani Farmers Co-operative Society Limited (in liquidation) (CS/2155) for a period not exceeding one year and whereas the said (1) Peter I. Kimotho, Chief Co-operative Officer, Headquarters, (2) Suleiman Wandati, Legal Officer, Headquarters, have not been able to complete the liquidation.
Now therefore, I extend the period of the liquidation with effect from 4th August, 2015, for another period of not exceeding one (1) year for the said (1) Peter I. Kimotho, Chief Co-operative Officer, Headquarters, (2) Suleiman Wandati, Legal Officer, Headquarters, to act as liquidators in the matter of the said co-operative society.
Dated the 4th August, 2015.
P. L. M. MUSYIMI,
MR/7769892              Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
