IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 9 (3) of the Alcoholic Drinks Control Act, 2010, the Naivasha Sub-County Alcoholics Drinks Regulation Committee, publishes the list of applicants for Alcoholic Drinks licence.
Any person with any objection to any of the applications can present it in writing to the Secretary Naivasha Sub-County Committee and serve notice of objection on the applicant personally or by post at least seven days before the hearing of the applications.
Applicants are advised to appear before the District Alcoholic Drinks Regulation Committee in person or be represented by an advocate.
