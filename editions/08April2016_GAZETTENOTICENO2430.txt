THE COUNTY GOVERNMENT OF ELGEYO/MARAKWET
SUSPENSION OF COMMERCIAL PRODUCTION AND TRANSPORTATION OF CHARCOAL IN ELGEYO/MARAKWET
PARSUANT to the provisions of the Environmental Management and Co-ordination (Amendment) Act, 2015 (No. 5 of 2015) as read with the principal Environmental Management and Co-ordination Act, 1999 (No. 8 of 1999) and the Forests Act, 2005 (Cap. 385), the Elgeyo/Marakwet Environment Committee issues the following notice:
That charcoal production and transportation for commercial purposes within Elgeyo/Marakwet County is suspended indefinitely until a substantive County legislation is passed to regulate the process.
That any transportation of charcoal sourced from neighbouring Counties shall only be done upon obtaining consent from the County Environment Committee.
That any person found to be producing and/or transporting charcoal for commercial purposes during the suspension period shall be deemed to have committed a general offence under the principal
Act and shall be liable to the general penalty under section 144 of the Principal Act. 
That this Notice is informed by and a result of the following grounds.
The wanton and unregulated charcoal production and transportation has resulted in gross environment degradation resulting in severe water shortage and reduced agricultural productivity.
The local communities have been exploited by being paid negligible fees for their labour and compensation for their roads access and resources.
Dated the 1st February, 2016.
S. KIPLAGAT,
