EXTRAORDINARY TARIFF ADJUSTMENT EXTENSION IN NORTHERN WATER SERVICES BOARD (NWSB) AREA
THE Water Services Regulatory Board has approved an extraordinary tariff extension for water and sewerage tariffs for implementation by selected Water Service Providers of Northern WSB in line with Water Act, 2002, section 73 (5).
By extension therefore, Northern WSB gives a one month notice to all existing and potential water and sewerage customers of the following listed Water Service Providers and their schemes that the current extra ordinary tariffs have been extended with effect from 1st January, 2011 and will be reviewed on January, 1st 2012 for further adjustment.
