THE WATER ACT
(NO. 8 OF 2002)
APPROVED TARIFF STRUCTURE FOR THE PERIOD
 2014/2015, 2015/16 AND 2016/17
THE Water Services Regulatory Board has pursuant to sections 47 (g) and 73 (5) of the Water Act, 2002 approved the proposed Water and Sewerage tariffs for implementation by Mavoko Water and Sewerage Company (MAVWASCO).
By extension therefore, Tanathi Water Services Board (TAWSB) gives a one month notice to all existing and potential Water and Sewerage customers of Mavoko Water and Sewerage Company and its environs that the tariff has been adjusted for the period 2014/2015, 2015/2016 and 2016/2017.
As per the Service Provision Agreement (SPA) between TAWSB and MAVWASCO which is approved by the Water Services Regulatory Board the areas of Kitengela, Mavoko Municipality which includes Githunguri, Syokimau, Katani, Joska, Mlolongo, Daystar/ Lukenya/ Kinanie, Kasina, Nzoiani, Manzoni, Kyumvi, Athi river town and all industries along Mombasa road from Kapa to Kyumvi, shall pay the approved Water and Sewerage tariff as per the terms of this notice.
