KENYA SCHOOL OF LAW
NOTIFICATION OF ATP SUPPLEMENTARY EXAMINATION DATES
 
PURSUANT to the Kenya School of Law Act, 2012, it is notified for general information that the Kenya School of Law Supplementary examinations for the Advocates Training Programme (ATP) shall be held at the Kenya School of Law, Lang’ata South Road, Karen, Nairobi, from 21st July, 2014 to 31st July, 2014, both dates inclusive.
