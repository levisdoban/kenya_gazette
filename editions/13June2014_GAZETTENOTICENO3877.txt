THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
DECLARATION OF VACANCY—GATUNDU SOUTH CONSTITUENCY
 
IT IS notified for general information that, pursuant to the provisions of Article 103 (1) (a) of the Constitution of Kenya, 2010, the seat in the National Assembly for Gatundu South Constituency has become vacant.
Dated the 10th June, 2014.
JUSTIN MUTURI,
                                                 Speaker of the National Assembly.
