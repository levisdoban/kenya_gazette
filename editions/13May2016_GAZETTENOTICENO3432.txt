THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(Cap. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
(Extension Order)
WHEREAS  by extension order dated the 8th March, 2015, I appointed Stanley W. Ongeti, Senior Assistant Commissioner for Co-operative Development, to be liquidator of Lukenya Ranching and Farming Co-operative Society Limited (in Liquidation (SC/961) for a period not exceeding one year and whreas the said Senior Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development has not been able complete the liquidation:
Now therefore, I  extend the period  of the liquidation with effect  from   8th March,  2016  for  another  period  not exceeding  one  year  for  the  said Senior Assistant Commissioner of Co-operative Development to  act  as liquidator in the matter of  the  said  Co-operative  Society.
P. N.  GICHUKI,
MR/9370570    Ag.  Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
