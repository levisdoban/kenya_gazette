Nominated Candidates for Member of National Assembly- Bonchari Constituency And Members of County Assembly- Kinakomba, Misikhu and Maua By-Elections
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 84, 85, 88 (4), 97 (1) (a), 99, 101(4) (b), 177 (1) (a), 193 and 194 of the Constitution of Kenya, section 4 (d), (j), (k) of the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission Act, 2011, sections 13 (3), 16, 22, 24, 25, 26, 31, 32 and 33 of the Elections Act, 2011 Part III, V and VIII and Regulations 51 (2), (5) and (6) of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012 the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission gives notice to the public that the persons listed in the 1st  and 2nd Column to the First, Second, Third and Fourth Schedules stand validly nominated for the Member of National Assembly- Bonchari Constituency: and Members of the Tana River, Bungoma and Meru County Assemblies; Kinakomba, Misikhu and Maua Wards respectively, By-elections to be held on 23rd June, 2014.
