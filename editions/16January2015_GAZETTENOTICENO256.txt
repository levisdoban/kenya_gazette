THE POLITICAL PARTIES ACT
(No. 11 of 2011)
Change of Political Party Constitution
IN EXERCISE of the power conferred by section 20 (1) (a) of the Political Parties Act, 2011, the Registrar of Political Parties gives notice that Orange Democratic Movement intends to change or amend its Constitution.
Further enquiries can be made through the Registrar of Political Parties offices, 1st Floor, Lion Place, Waiyaki Way, and P.O. Box 1131-00606, Nairobi,  from 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m.
Dated the 15th of January 2015.
LUCY K. NDUNGU,
MR/6742109           Registrar of Political Parties.
