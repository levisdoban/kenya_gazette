THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Murigu s/o Waiguru, of P.O. Box 344, Nyeri in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Tetu/Kiriti/234, situate in the district of Nyeri, and whereas the High Court at Nyeri in succession cause No. 1412 of 2012, has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Peter Muriithi Murigu, both of P.O. Box 344, Nyeri, and whereas the registered proprietor has refused to surrender the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and issue a land title deed to the said Peter Muriithi Murigu, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Murigu s/o Waiguru, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 16th October, 2015.
R. W. NGAANYI,
