THE LABOUR RELATIONS ACT
(No. 14 of 2007)
AMENDMENT OF THE CONSTITUTION AND RULES AND CHANGE OF NAME OF THE UNION
NOTICE is given to all members of the Kenya Union of Employees of Polytechnics, Colleges and Allied Institutions pursuant to section 27 (4) of the Labour Relations Act, that a notice of change of the Constitution and a notice of change of name of the Union has been received.
The Union intends to change its name from Kenya Union of Employees of Polytechnics, Colleges and Allied Institutions to Kenya Tertiary and Schools Workers’ Union.
Any person or member intending to raise any objection against either the amendment of the constitution or change of name of the union is required to submit in writing any objections against the amendment of the constitution or change of name of the union within twenty one (21) days from the date hereof. The amendments are open for scrutiny from the undersigned’s offices during working hours.
E. N. GICHEHA,
MR/8449331                                         Registrar of Trade Unions.
