THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(NO. 3 OF 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS John Wanjohi, is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Sipili/Donyoloip Block 1/4309 (Laikipia), situate in Laikipia District, and whereas sufficient evidence adduced proves that the said land title deed was erroneously issued, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the said land title deed for cancellation have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received, I intend to dispense with production of the said land title deed and give a new title deed to the rightful owner, Peter Mwangi Wambugu, and upon such publication the land title issued earlier shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 17th October, 2014.
B.W.MWAI,
MR/3834306                                                          Land Registrar. Laikipia District.
