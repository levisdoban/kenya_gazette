THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(CAP. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
(EXTENSION ORDER)
WHEREAS by order dated 13th May, 2010, I, appointed L. M. Magaju, District Co-operative Auditor, Isiolo District, to be liquidator of New Mugoiri FCS (in liquidation) (CS/4647), for a period not exceeding one year and whereas the said L. M. Magaju, has not been able to complete the liquidation;
Now therefore, I, extended the period of the liquidation with effect from 14th May, 2011, for another period not exceeding one year for the said L. M. Magaju, to act as liquidator in the matter of the said co-operative society.
Dated the 14th May, 2011.
F. F. ODHIAMBO,
MR7363988               Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
