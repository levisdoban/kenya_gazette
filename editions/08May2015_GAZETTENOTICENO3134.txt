Appointment

Riechi Stephen Nyangau,
Sawe Olga Akech,
Winfrida Adhiambo Okwany,
Otieno Patrick Okwara,
Anthony Kimani Ndung’u,

Dated the 5th May, 2015.
 
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
