CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
MACHAKOS COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTINGS
PURSUANT to Standing Order No. 26 (1)–(3) of the Machakos County Assembly Standing Orders, it is notified for general information of the members of the public and members of the County Assembly of Machakos that there shall be a special sitting of the county assembly to be held on 20th April, 2015, at the assembly chambers at County Hall, along Mwatu wa Ngoma Road, at 10.00 a.m.
The business to be transacted during the special sitting shall be Machakos County Supplementary Budget and Appropriation Bill, 2014/2015.
Dated the 16th  April, 2015.
BERNARD MUNG’ATA,
MR/7407296                                           Speaker of the Assembly.
