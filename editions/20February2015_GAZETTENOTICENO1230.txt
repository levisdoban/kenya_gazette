THE MEDICAL LABORATORY TECHNICIANS AND TECHNOLOGISTS ACT
(Cap. 253A)
Appointment of Board Members
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) of the Medical Laboratory Technicians and Technologists Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Health appoints—
Under paragraph (i)—
Benadette Tipina Nkukuu,
Waithaka Kinge (Dr.),
Everlyne Nyawira Macharia,
to be members of the Kenya Medical Laboratory Technicians and Technologists Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 12th February, 2015 and revokes the appointment* of Esther Mwau, Valentine Magero and Wilson M. Gichimba.
Gazette Notice No. 12432 of 2012, is cancelled.
Dated the 13th February, 2015.
JAMES W. MACHARIA,
Cabinet Secretary for Health.
*G.N. 12431/2012.
