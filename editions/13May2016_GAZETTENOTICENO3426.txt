THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENT ACT
THE NYANDARUA COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE ASSEMBLY
NOTICE is given to all Members of the County Assembly and the public that pursuant to Article 29 (1) and (2) of the County Assembly Standing Orders, Special Sittings of the County Assembly shall be held at the County Assembly Chambers PCEA Church Building, Ol Kalou on Wednesday, 18th May, 2016 and on Friday, 20th May, 2016 at 10.00am. At the Special Sittings, the following businesses shall be transacted:
1.  Wednesday the 18th day of May, 2016 at 10.00 a.m.
(a) The Nyandarua County Trade Fund Regulations 2016;
(b) The Nyandarua County 2nd Supplementary Budget Estimates in the Financial Year 2015/2016; and
2.  Friday the 20th day of May 2016 at 10.00 a.m.
The Nyandarua County 2nd Supplementary Budget Estimates 2016 and the subsequent consideration and approval of the Supplementary Budget Estimates.
Dated the 11th  May 2016.
JAMES NDEGWA WAHOME,
MR/9370802                                Speaker to the County Assembly
