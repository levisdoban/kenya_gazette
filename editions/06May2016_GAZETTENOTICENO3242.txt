THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Samuel Maitha Muoki, of P.O. Box 1106, Kangundo in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 1.194 hectares or thereabout, known as Donyo Sabuk/Komarock Block I/3745, situate in the district of Machakos and whereas the High Court at Machakos in succession cause No. 547 of 2014, has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Syombua Sammy, of P.O. and the confirmation of the grant in favour of Syombua Sammy, of P.O. Box 1106, Kangundo, and upon such registration, the land title deed issued earlier to the said Samuel Maitha Muoki, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
