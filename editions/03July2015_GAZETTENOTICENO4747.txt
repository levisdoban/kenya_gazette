THE JUDICATURE ACT

THE HIGH COURT (ADMIRALTY) RULES. 1979
(L.N. 116 of 1979)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by rule  4 (2) of the High Court Admiralty Rules, 1979, the Chief Justice on the advice of the Judicial Service Commission appoints—
Julius Mukut Nangea,
Irene Ngotho Ruguru; and
Ivy Dorothy Wasike,
Deputy Registrars of the High Court, to be Admiralty Marshals, with effect from 23rd June, 2015.
The appointment of Joyce Mkambe Gandani vide GAZETTE NOTICE NO.  15061 of 2011, is revoked.
Dated the 30th June, 2015.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice/President Supreme Court of Kenya.
