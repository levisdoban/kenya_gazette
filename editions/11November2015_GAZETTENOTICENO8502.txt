JOMO KENYATTA UNIVERSITY OF AGRICULTURE AND TECHNOLOGY
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 36 (1) (a) of the Universities Act, 2012, the Cabinet Secretary for Education, Science and Technology appoints—
Paul W. Njuki Kanyari (Prof.)
to be the Chairman of the Council of Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, for a period of four (4) years, with effect from 9th November, 2015.
Dated the 9th November, 2015.
J. T. KAIMENYI,
Cabinet Secretary,
Education, Science and Technology.
