THE KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY
THE INTERIM KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
Special Sitting of the Kiambu County Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Order 26 (3) of the Standing Orders of the Kiambu County Assembly, it is notified for the information of Members of the Kiambu County that a special sitting of the Assembly shall be held in the Kiambu County Chamber in the Main Assembly Buildings, Kiambu, on Friday, 30th January, 2015 at 2.30 p.m. for purposes of tabling of the Kiambu County Supplementary Budget for the year ending 30th June, 2015.
Dated the 28th January, 2015.
GATHII IRUNGU,
Speaker.
