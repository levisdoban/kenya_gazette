GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 5709
THE KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY
THE INTERIM KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to Standing Order 26 (3) of the Standing Orders of the Kiambu County Assembly, it is notified for the information of members of the Kiambu County that a special sitting of the Assembly shall be held in the Kiambu County Chamber in the Main Assembly Buildings, Kiambu, on Saturday, 16th August, 2014, at 9.00 a.m., for purposes of consideration of Kiambu County Appropriation Bill, 2014.
Dated the 15th August, 2014.
GATHII IRUNGU,
Speaker.
