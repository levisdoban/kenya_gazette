THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Ngugi Kamau Gatuha (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 17.095 hectares or thereabout, known as Nyandarua/Muruaki/4471, situate in the district of Nyandarua, and whereas the High Court at Naivasha in succession cause No. 58 of 2008, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) Milka Wanjiru Ngugi and (2) Daniel Kamau Ngugi, of the estate of Ngugi Kamau Gatuha (deceased), and whereas the land title deed issued earlier to the said Ngugi Kamau Gatuha (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.l. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Ngugi Kamau Gatuha (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 23rd October, 2015.
C. M. GICHUKI,
MR/8220915         Land Registrar, Nyandarua/Samburu Districts.
