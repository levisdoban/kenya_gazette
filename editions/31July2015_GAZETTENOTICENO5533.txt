THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COURT OF APPEAL
SUMMER VACATION 2015
THE Summer Vacation shall commence on Monday the 3rd August, 2015 and shall terminate on Tuesday, the 15th September, 2015. During the Vacation, the Registry of the Court will be open to the public from 9.00 a.m. to 12 noon on all weekdays except public holidays. A judge will be in attendance for the disposal of any urgent business.
Dated the 23rd July, 2015.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice/President of the Supreme Court of Kenya.
