THE UNIVERSITIES ACT
(No. 42 of 2012)
TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY OF KENYA
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 36 (1) (a) of the Universities Act, 2012, the Cabinet Secretary for Education, Science and Technology appoints—
Godfrey Mbiti Nguru (Prof.)
to be the Chairman of the Council of Technical University of Kenya, for a period of four (4) years, with effect from 9th November, 2015.
Dated the 9th November, 2015.
J. T. KAIMENYI,
Cabinet Secretary,
Education, Science and Technology.
