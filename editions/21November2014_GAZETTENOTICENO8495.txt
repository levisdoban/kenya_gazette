THE TRANSFER OF BUSINESS ACT
(Cap. 500)
BUSINESS TRANSFER
NOTICE is given pursuant to section 3 of the Transfer of Business Act, that Mobile for Good Kenya Limited, of P.O. Box 54529–00100 Nairobi in the Republic of Kenya (“the transferor”) carrying on in Kenya, as part of its business activities, the business of an online employment search and matching software engine under the name “KAZI 560” (“the business asset”) has agreed to transfer the business asset to Zetu Kenya Limited, of P.O. Box 21222 – 00100, Nairobi in the Republic of Kenya (“the transferee). The transfer took place on 25th July, 2014.
The transfer will result in the business previously carried on by Mobile For Good Limited using the business asset being carried on by Zetu Kenya Limited using the business asset. The transfer will result in all contracts relating to the transferred business asset being transferred to the transferee notwithstanding any restrictions on the transfer or requirements for counter party consent and without triggering any pre-emption, termination or other rights, which such contracts may contain.
The transferee shall assume all the debts and liabilities relating to the business asset incurred by the transferor.
Dated the 7th Novenber, 2014.
