REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Nahashon Gachoki Mugi (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Mutira/Kirunda/883, situate in the district of Kirinyaga, and whereas the judge of the High Court at Nairobi in succession case No. 59 of 2007, has issued grant of letters of administration to Robert Munene Gachoki, and whereas all efforts made to recover the land title deed and be surrendered to the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said administration letters to Rober Munene Gachoki, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Nahashon Gachoki Mugi (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
