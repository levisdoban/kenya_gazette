THE KENYA WATER TOWERS AGENCY ORDER, 2012
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Paragraph 6 (1) (g) of the Kenya Water Towers Agency Order, 2012, the Cabinet Secretary for  Environment and Natural Resources appoints—
Bifwoli Wakoli,
Robert Kipchumba Togogo,

Dated the 2nd October, 2015.
JUDI WAKHUNGU,
