THE  CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(Cap. 490)
CO-OPERATIVE TRIBUNAL SESSIONS
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 78 (3) of the Co-operative Societies Act, revised, 2005, the Chairman, Co-operative Tribunal notifies the general public of the Tribunal’s calendar of sittings outside Nairobi, which has beeen amended in the following counties, during the financial year 2015/2016 as follows:
The Mombasa sitting that was listed for 15th to 19th February, 2016, has been amended with Nyeri for the same dates and the Nyeri sitting that was listed for 21st to 25th March, 2016 has been amended with Mombasa for the same dates.
Dated the 12th January, 2016.
ALEX ITHUKU,
Chairman, Co-operative Tribunal.
