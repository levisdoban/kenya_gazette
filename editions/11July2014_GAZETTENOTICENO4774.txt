CHANGE OF NAME
NOTICE is given that by a deed poll dated 11th June, 2014, duly executed and registered in the Registry of Documents at Nairobi as Presentation No. 120, in Volume DI, Folio 134/42044, File No. MMXIV, by our client, Yasmin Mohamed Ahmed, of Chief Tarbaj Location Spinster, in the Republic of Kenya, formerly known as Mandek Mohamed Ahmed, formally and absolutely renounced and abandoned the use of her former name Mandek Mohamed Ahmed and in lieu thereof assumed and adopted the name Yasmin Mohamed Ahmed, for all purposes and authorizes and requests all persons at all times to designate, describe and address her by her assumed name Yasmin Mohamed Ahmed only.
