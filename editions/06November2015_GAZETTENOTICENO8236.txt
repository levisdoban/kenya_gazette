THE SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY AND INNOVATION ACT
(No. 28 of 2013)
THE KENYA NATIONAL INNOVATION AGENCY
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 28 (3) (a) of the Science, Technology and Innovation Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Education, Science and Technology appoints—
Reuben O. Marwanga (Prof.)
to be the Chairman of the Kenya National Innovation Agency, for  a period of three (3) years, with effect from 5th October, 2015.
Dated the 26th October, 2015.
JACOB T. KAIMENYI,
Cabinet Secretary for Education,
Science and Technology.
