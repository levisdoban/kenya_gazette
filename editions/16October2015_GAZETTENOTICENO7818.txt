NOTICE TO DEPOSITORS AND CREDITORS OF COMMENCEMENT TO MAKE 5TH PAYMENT
NOTICE is given that the Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation (KDIC), in exercise of the powers conferred under rule 107 of the Companies (Winding up) rules, will commence payment of the 5th dividend from 7th October, 2015 to all eligible depositors and creditors of Trust Bank Ltd (In Liquidation) who have proved their claims.
All payments shall be made through Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT). In this regard, depositors and creditors who have not provided their current Bank Account details should furnish the Liquidation Agent with the same at the offices of Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation, located on the 2nd Floor, CBK Pension House (Formerly Marshall House), Harambee Avenue, Nairobi, between 9.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. from Monday to Friday.
The payment shall be distributed on a pro-rata basis taking into account claimant’s outstanding balance upto a maximum of KSh.100,000 plus 12.4% of the remaining balance, if any.
Claimants are further advised to note that this payment is valid for a period of one (1) year from the date of commencement.
Dated the 1st October, 2015.

Ag. Chief Executive Officer,
