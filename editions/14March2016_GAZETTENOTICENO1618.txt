GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 1618
THE PREVENTION OF TERRORISM ACT
(No. 30 of 2012)
DECLARATION OF SPECIFIED ENTITIES
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (3) of the Prevention of Terrorism Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government declares the following entities to be specified entities for purposes of the Act:
1.     Al-Shabaab
2.     Al-Qaeda
3.     Boko Haram
4.     Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS)
5.     Mombasa Republican Council (MRC)
6.     Mohamed Kuno Dulyadeen alias Gamadhere
7.     Abdifatah Abubakar Abdi alias Muhajir
8.     Ahmed Iman Ali alias Abu Zunira
9.     Faud Abubakar Abdi alias Faud Manswab
10.   Erick Achayo Ogada alias Ibra Salim Erico
11.   Abdikadir Mohamed Abdikadir alias Ikrima
12.   Maalim Abbas Guyo
Dated the 14th March, 2016.
JOSEPH NKAISSERY,
Cabinet Secretary for Interior
and Co-ordination of National Government.
