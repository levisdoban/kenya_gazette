THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
 (Cap. 446)
THE GEOTHERMAL DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED
Revocation of Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by sections 6 (1) (e) of the State Corporations Act, the Acting Cabinet Secretary for Energy and Petroleum revokes the appointment* of—
Joseph Ichunge Kinyua*,
James Owino Okwero (Eng.)†,
as members of the Board of the Geothermal Development Company Limited, with effect from 16th April, 2015.
Dated the 16th April, 2015.
HENRY ROTICH,
                          Ag. Cabinet Secretary for Energy and Petroleum.
*G.N. 1090/2013 and †G.N. 16273/2012.
