THE REGISTERED LAND ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Marete Makinu (deceased), is the registered proprietor of all that piece of land known as Abothuguchi/Makandune/451, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the of the High Court in succession cause No. 39 of 2012, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant to Catherine Kathoni Marete, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said application by transmission R.L. 19 in the name of Catherine Kathoni Marete, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Marete Makinu (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
