THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS M’Muronga M’Arimi (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Ntima/Ntakira/1878, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court in succession cause No. 624 of 2009, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of (1) Richard Murithi and (2) Samwel Karonga Mukira, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the land title deed in respect of M’Muronga M’Arimi (deceased), the said piece of land registered in the name of is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in the names of (1) Richard Murithi and (2) Samwel Karonga Mukira, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said M’Muronga M’Arimi (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
