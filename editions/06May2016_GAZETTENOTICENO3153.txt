THE CENTRAL BANK OF KENYA ACT
(Cap. 491)
SPECIFICATION OF INSTITUTION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 38 (6) (c) of the Central Bank of Kenya Act, the Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury, on the recommendation of the Central Bank of Kenya, specifies “a microfinance bank licensed under the Microfinance Bank Act, 2006”, to be an institution for the purposes of section 38.
Dated the 29th March, 2016.
HENRY K. ROTICH,
Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury.
