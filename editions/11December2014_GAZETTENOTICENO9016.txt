
				Page: 3331GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 9016
ELECTIONS ACT
(No. 24 of 2011)
TO: THE INDEPENDENT ELECTORAL AND BOUNDARIES COMMISSION
FOR HOMA BAY COUNTY
WHEREAS a vacancy has arisen in the office of a member of the Senate in respect of the above-mentioned county:
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of the provisions of section 16 (3) of the Elections Act, 2011, I command you that, due notice being first given, you do cause election to be held according to law of a member to serve in the Senate for the said county.
Dated the 10th December, 2014.
EKWEE ETHURO,
Speaker of the Senate.
