Special Sitting of the Kiambu County Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Order 26 (3) of the Standing Orders of the Kiambu County Assembly, it is notified for the information of Members of the Kiambu County Assembly that a special sitting of the Assembly shall be held in the Kiambu County Assembly Chamber in the Main Assembly Buildings, Kiambu, on Saturday, 15th November, 2014 at 10.00 p.m. for purposes of consideration of the Kiambu County Finance Bill, 2014.
