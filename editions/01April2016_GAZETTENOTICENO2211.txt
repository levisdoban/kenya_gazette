GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 2211
THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
RECONSTRUCTION OF LOST OR DESTROYED LAND REGISTER
WHEREAS Novillera Company Limited, a limited liability company incorporated in Kenya, of P.O. Box 40092, Nairobi, is registered as proprietor of all that piece of land known as L.R. No. 13669, situate in the city of Nairobi in the Nairobi Area, by virtue of a certificate of title registered as I.R. 46553, and whereas the land register in respect thereof is lost or destroyed, and whereas efforts made to locate the said land register have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of sixty (60) days from the date hereof, the property register shall be reconstructed under the provisions of section 33 (5) of the Act, provided that no objection has been received within that period.
Dated the 1st April, 2016.
G. M. MUYANGA,
MR/8858279                                           Land Registrar, Nairobi.
