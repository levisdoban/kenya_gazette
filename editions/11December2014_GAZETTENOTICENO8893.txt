THE TASKFORCE ON TOURISM RECOVERY
Extension of Period of Appointment
IT IS notified for the general information of the public that the period of appointment of the Taskforce on Tourism Recovery, contained in Gazette Notice No. 4611 of 2014, has been extended for a period of three months, with effect from the 24th October, 2014.
Dated the 24th November, 2014.
PHYLLIS J. KANDIE,
Cabinet Secretary, 
Ministry of East African Affairs, Commerce and Tourism.
