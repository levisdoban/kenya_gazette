THE CIVIL PROCEDURE ACT
(CAP.  21)
ESTABLISHMENT
TAKE NOTICE that the Chief Justice/President, Supreme Court of Kenya has established Loitoktok Law Courts, a magistrate’s court that is within the supervisory Jurisdiction of Kajiado High Court, with effect from the 6th June, 2016.
Dated the 5th May, 2016.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief justice and president, Supreme Court of Kenya.
