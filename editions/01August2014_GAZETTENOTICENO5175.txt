MAKUENI COUNTY ASSEMBLY
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to the Standing Orders of the Makueni County Assembly, it is notified for the information of the Members of the County Assembly that the Assembly shall hold a special sitting on Monday, 28th July, 2014 at 8.30 a.m. for the purpose of holding elections for Deputy Speaker of the County Assembly.
Dated the 24th July, 2014
STEPHEN NGELU,
Speaker of the County Assembly.
