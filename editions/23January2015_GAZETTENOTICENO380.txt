Gazette Notice No. 380
THE EAST AFRICAN COMMUNITY CUSTOMS MANAGEMENT ACT, 2004
Revocation of Appointment
IN ACCORDANCE with the powers conferred by section 12 (3) of the East African Community Customs Management Act, 2004, the Commissioner of Customs Services revokes the appointment of the premises of `Kipevu Inland Container Depot Limited as a transit shed,
Gazette Notice No. 1648 of 2001, is revoked.
Dated the 15th January, 2015.
BEATRICE MEMO,
Commissioner, Customs Services Department.
