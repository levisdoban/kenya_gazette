THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT
(CAP. 4295)
J.K.I.A.–MUSEUM–GIGIRI ROAD PROJECT
WITHDRAWAL FROM ACQUISITION
IN PURSUANCE of section 23 (1) of the Land Acquisition Act, the Commissioner of Lands gives notice that the Government has withdrawn from the acquisition of land hitherto required for the J.K.I.A.–Museum–Gigiri Road Project and subject to Gazette Notice No. 16756 and 16757 of 2010.
Accordingly, the above Gazette Notices are revoked.
Plans for the affected land may be inspected during office hours at the office of the Commissioner of Lands, Ardhi House, Nairobi.
Dated the 6th April, 2011.
Z. A. MABEA,
MR1681747                                             Commissioner of Lands.
