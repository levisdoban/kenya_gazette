THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE LAIKIPIA COUNTY SERVICE BOARD
REPORT FOR THE YEAR 2014
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred under section 59 of the County Governments Act, which sets out the functions and powers of the County Public Service Board, one of such function being preparation of regular reports for submission to the County Assembly on execution of the functions of the board, the board announces the completion and submission of the year 2014 Report to the County Assembly of Laikipia.
Further, in compliance to section 59 (b) of the County Governments Act, publishes a statement on the completion and notification to all the stakeholder and interested parties to access the said 2014 Annual Report in the County Government of Laikipia website.www.laikipiacounty.go.ke
Comments reactions to be forwarded to the Secretary, Laikipia County Public Service Board, P.O. Box 52–20300, Nyahururu or through E-mail address cpsb@laikipiacounty.go.ke or hand delivered to the board’s office at Town Hall, Nyahururu, Room No. 14.
By order of the Laikipia County Public Service Board.
Dated the 31st December, 2014.
G. M. MWANGI,
Secretary/CEO,
MR/7828860                     Laikipia County Public Service Board.
