THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 79 (2) )
RECTIFICATION OF A REGISTER
WHEREAS Winifred Wangu Wanjiku, of P.O. Box 133 Karatina, is registered as proprietor in absolute ownership interest of that piece of land containing 1.348 hectares or thereabout, situate in the district of Nyeri, registered under title No. Naromoro/Block 2/Muriru/979, having acquired the same from one John Kiondo Mwaniki, and whereas all evidence adduced proves that the said land was acquired fraudulently, and whereas all efforts made to compel the said Winifred Wangu Wanjiku to surrender the said title for cancellation have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of sixty (60) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been raised, I shall dispense with the production of the said title deed and proceed to rectify the title register to read Kiondo Mwaniki, and upon such rectification, the said title deed issued to Winifred Wangu Wanjiku, shall be deemed to have been cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 21st June, 2013.
