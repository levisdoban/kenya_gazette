TURKANA COUNTY GOVERNMENT
TURKANA COUNTY ASSEMBLY
SPECIAL SITTING
PURSUANT to Standing Order No. 19 and 27 of the Turkana County Assembly Standing Orders, it is notified for the information of members of the County Assembly of Turkana and the general public that the Assembly shall have a special sitting on Thursday, 28th April, 2016, as from 2.30 p.m. at the County Assembly Hall in Lodwar. The main business will be State of the County Address by the Governor.
Dated the 18th April, 2016.
GEOFFREY E. KAITUKO,
MR/9327277                         Speaker, Turkana County Assembly.
