
				Page: 2624GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 8230
THE INCOME TAX ACT
(Cap. 470)
AUTHORIZATION OF REPRESENTATIVE OF COMPETENT AUTHORITY
IT IS notified for general information that the Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury, being the competent authority for the Agreements entered under sections 41 and 41A of the Act, has appointed the Commissioner-General of the Kenya Revenue Authority as the authorized representative of the competent authority for the purposes of exchange of information on tax matters.
The Commissioner-General shall act in consultation with the Cabinent Secretary in the performance of the functions attendant to this authorization.
Dateed the 16th October, 2015.
HENRY K. ROTICH,
Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury.
