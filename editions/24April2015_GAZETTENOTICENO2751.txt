THE ADVOCATES ACT
THE COMPLAINTS COMMISSION
97TH QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT to section 53 (9) of the Advocates Act and Rule 12 (1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st January, 2015 to 31st March, 2015, the Commission received a total of one hundred and eighty eight (188) new complaints out of which thirty five (35) were classified and files opened. One hundred and fifty three (153) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
The nature of new complaints is as shown below:

(a) Failure to account                                                        2
