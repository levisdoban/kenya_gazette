THE COMPANIES ACT

APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by sections 7 (3) of the State Corporations Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces of the Republic of Kenya, appoint—
Gershom Otachi
to be the chairperson of  Geothermal Development Company up to 19th May,  2015 with effect from the 17th April, 2015 and revoke the appointment of Faisal Abass*.
Dated the 17th April, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
