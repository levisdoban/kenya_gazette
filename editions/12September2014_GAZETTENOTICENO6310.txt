THE NATIONAL FLAG, EMBLEMS AND NAMES ACT
(CAP. 99)
GRANT OF PERMISSION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (1) of the National Flag. Emblems and Names Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government grants permission to the National Youth Service to use the image of the National Flag Shield or its likeness in the National Youth Service Emblem.
Dated the 9th September. 2014.
JOSEPH ole LENKU,
Cabinet Secretary,
Ministry of Interior and Co-ordination of National Government.
