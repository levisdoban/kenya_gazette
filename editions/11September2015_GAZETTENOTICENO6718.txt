THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(Cap. 490)
Liquidation/Cancellation Order
WHEREAS I am of the opinion that Hisa Sacco Society Limited (CS/11470) should be dissolved pursuant to section 62 (1) (b) and (c) of the said Act, I cancel the registration of the said society and order that it be liquidated and take notice that the order shall take effect immediately.
Any member of the society may within thirty (30) days of this order appeal to the Cabinet Secretary responsible for co-operatives against the order.
And further, pursuant to section 65 of the said Act, I appoint Jane M. Ngaruiya, Senior Co-operative Officer, P.O. Box 30202, Nairobi, to be liquidator for a period not exceeding one (1) year and authorize her to take into her custody all the properties of the said society including such books and documents as are deemed necessary for completion of the liquidation.
Dated the 12th March, 2015.
P. L. M. MUSYIMI,
MR/8045628            Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
