THE ENERGY ACT
(No. 12 of 2006)
THE RURAL ELECTRIFICATION AUTHORITY
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Section 68 (1) (a) of the Energy Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces appoint—
Simon gicharu
to be the Chairperson of the Rural Electrification Authority Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 28th May, 2015.
Dated the 4th June, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
