THE ELECTION (GENERAL) REGULATIONS, 2012
By-Election for Makueni County Assembly—Masongaleni Ward and Bomet County Assembly—Nyangores Ward Scheduled to be Held on 12th February, 2016
 IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 84, 85, 88 (4), 177, 193 and 194 (a) of the Constitution of Kenya, sections 13 (3), 19, 22, 25, 26, 29, 30, 32, 33, 38 and 74 of the Elections Act, and Regulations 11 (3) and 12 (1), and Part III and VIII of the Elections (General) Regulations, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission gives notice that:
(a) Vacancies occurred in the offices of the Member of Makueni County Assembly—Masongaleni Ward and Bomet County Assembly—Nyangores Ward on 27th September, 2015 and 11th October, 2015, respectively.
(b) Independent candidates to submit their symbols on or beforeWednesday, 23rd December, 2015 being 21 days before the Commission nomination days.
Each political party wishing to participate in the elections must finalize the nominations and determine intra-party disputes
(a) arising from party nomination of its candidate(s) on or before the Tuesday, 29th December, 2015.
(b) Submission of the list and details of the political party nominated candidates and the independent candidates to the Commission shall be on Friday, 8th January, 2016.
(c)  Disputes relating to or arising from nominations shall be determined within seven (7) days of lodging the dispute(s) with the Commission.
(d) The days for the nomination of political parties candidates and independent candidates for the by-election will be on Wednesday, 13th January, 2016 and Thursday, 14th January, 2016 and nomination papers shall be delivered by the candidates to the Returning Officer between the hours of eight o’clock in the morning and four o’clock in the afternoon with an hour break between one o’clock and two o’clock.
(e)  Disputes relating to or arising from the decisions of the Returning Officer after nominations shall be determined within seven days of lodging the dispute(s) with the Commission.
(f)   Pursuant to the provisions of regulation 57 (1) and (2) of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012 every political party and every independent candidate at the by-election shall submit to the Commission names of the Chief Agents on or before 29th January, 2016.
(g) The campaign period for purposes of the 12th February, 2016, by-elections shall commence on 15th January, 2016 and cease on 10th February, 2016 being 48 hours before the by-election day.
(a) The campaign time shall run from 7.00 a.m. to 6.00 p.m. during the campaign period.
(b) If the by-elections are contested, the poll will take place on the 12th February, 2016.
Notes:
The attention of candidates and persons subscribing nomination papers is drawn to the Provisions of VIII of the Election (General) Regulations on the County Assembly Elections (Subsidiary Legislation), First Schedule to the Leadership and Integrity Act No. 19 of 2012 and Chapter Six of the Constitution of Kenya.
Every political party and every person who participates in the by- election shall subscribe to and observe the Electoral Code of Conduct set out in the Second Schedule to the Elections Act, 2011.
Pursuant to the provisions of section 26 of the Elections Act, 2011 any candidate who intends to vie for the by-election should not participate in public fundraising.
NB.—This Gazette Notice revokes GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 9117 of 2015 Vol. CXVII–No. 135 dated the 3rd December, 2015 and published on 9th  December, 2015.
