GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 4349
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE ELECTIONS ACT
(No. 24 of 2011)
THE ELECTION (GENERAL) REGULATIONS, 2012
BY-ELECTION FOR UGENYA NORTH WARD, SIAYA COUNTY
 IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 84, 85, 88(4), 177, 193, 194(a) of the Constitution of Kenya, sections 13 (3), 19, 22, 25, 26, 29,30, 32,33, 38, 74 of the Elections Act, and Regulations 11(3), 12 (1),15(1) (a), Part III and VIII of the Elections (General) Regulations, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission gives notice that:
(a) A vacancy occurred in the office of the Member of Siaya County Assembly for Ugenya North Ward on 12th March, 2015.
(b)  Independent Candidates to submit their symbols on or before 29th June, 2015 being 21 days before the Commission nomination days.
(c)   Each Political Party wishing to participate in the elections must finalize the nominations and determine intra-party disputes arising from party nomination of its candidate(s) on or before the 3rd July, 2015.
(d)  Submission of the list and details of the Political Party nominated candidates and the Independent candidates to the Commission shall be on 15th July, 2015.
(e)   Disputes relating to or arising from nominations shall be determined within seven (7) days of lodging the dispute(s) with the Commission which disputes must be lodged within 24 hours of occurrence.
(f)    The days for the nomination of Political Parties candidates and Independent Candidates for the By-Election will be on Monday, 20th July, 2015 and Tuesday, 21st July, 2015 and nomination papers shall be delivered by candidates to the Returning Officer between the hours of eight O’clock in the morning and four O’clock in the afternoon with a 1 hour break between 1.00 p.m. and 2.00 p.m.
(g)  Disputes relating to or arising from decisions of the Returning Officer after nominations shall be determined within seven (7) days of lodging the dispute(s) with the Commission which disputes must be lodged within 24 hours of occurrence.
(h)  Pursuant to the provisions of regulation 57 (1) and (2) of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012 every Political Party and every Independent Candidate at the by-election shall submit to the Commission names of the Chief Agents on or before 3rd August, 2015.
(i)    The Campaign period for purposes of the 17th August, 2015 By-Elections shall commence on 20th July, 2015 and cease on 15th August, 2015 being 48 hours before the By-Election Day.
(j)    The Campaign time shall run from 7.00 a.m. to 6.00 p.m. during the campaign period.
(k)   If the by-election is contested, the poll will take place on the 17th August, 2015.
Notes
(a) The attention of candidates and persons subscribing nomination papers is drawn to the provisions of PART VIII of the Election (General) Regulations on the County Assembly Elections (Subsidiary Legislation), First Schedule to the Leadership and Integrity Act No. 19 of 2012 and Chapter Six of the Constitution of Kenya.
(b) Every political party and every person who participates in the by- election shall subscribe to and observe the Electoral Code of Conduct set out in the Second Schedule to the Elections Act, 2011.
(c)  In pursuance to the provisions of Section 26 of the Elections Act, 2011 any candidate who intends to vie for the by-election should not participate in public fundraising.
Dated the 11th June 2015.
A. I. HASSAN,
Chairperson,
Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission.
