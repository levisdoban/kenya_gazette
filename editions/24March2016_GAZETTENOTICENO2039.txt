THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
SPECIAL SITTING OF PARLIAMENT
NOTICE is given to all Senators that pursuant to Article 132 (1) (c) (i) and (iii) of the Constitution of Kenya, 2010, His Excellency, Hon. Uhuru Kenyatta, C.G.H., President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, wishes to address a Special Sitting of Parliament on Thursday, 31st March, 2016, at 2.30 p.m.
Accordingly, I notify all Senators that a Special Sitting of Parliament shall be held on Thursday, 31st March, 2016, at 2.30 p.m. at the National Assembly Chamber, Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi.
Dated the 21st March, 2016.
EKWEE ETHURO,
speaker of the senate
