THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002, Section 107)
NOTICE OF PUBLIC CONSULTATION
NOTICE is given to the general public in the area of Athi Water Services Board (AWSB) which has a license to provide water services in the counties of Nairobi, Kiambu and Gatanga area of Muranga county has applied to the Water Services Regulatory board for a regular tariff adjustment for its agent Nairobi City Water and Sewerage company for the period 2014–2015 to 2017–2018. The water company serves the Nairobi Area.
AWSB proposes an upward tariff review to enable Nairobi City Water and Sewerage Company to attain full cost recovery, undertake investment and meet conditions for improving service delivery.
The AWSB proposes to adopt rising block tariff for all consumer categories except water kiosks. Details of the tariff can be obtained from www.awsboard.go.ke  or at the Nairobi City Water and Sewerage Company Kampala Road www.nairobiwater.co.ke
Written comments on service delivery and or Justified objections to the upward tariff review should be addressed to the: Chief Executive Officer, Athi Water Services Board, P.O. Box 45283-00100 Nairobi, info@awsboard.go.ke
The closing date for the comments shall be on 20th December, 2014.
A Public Consultation meeting shall be held on 10th December 2014 at Laico Regency starting at 9 a.m. All members of the public in that area of supply are invited to attend.
