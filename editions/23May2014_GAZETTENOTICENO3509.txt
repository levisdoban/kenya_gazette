
				Page: 1317GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 3509
IN THE HIGH COURT OF KENYA AT BUNGOMA
PROBATE AND ADMINISTRATION
TAKE NOTICE that an application having been made in this court in:
Cause No. 178 of 2013
By Inviolata Nawire Wanyanga, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Elias Waswa Werukha, who died at Nandi Hills, on 24th August, 2013.
Cause No. 357 of 2013
By Richard Nabusoba, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Aurellia Naliaka Nabusoba, who died at Bungoma, on 21st January, 2013.
Cause No. 169 of 2014
By (1) Agnes Nafula Sirengo and (2) Martin Mukelasia Sirengo, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Jodham Sirengo Mukelasia, who died at District Hospital, Bungoma in Kenya, on 23rd July, 2013.
Cause No. 179 of 2014
By (1) Judith Nafula Melabi and (2) Mary Nafuna Masaba, both of P.O. Box 621, Webuye in Kenya, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Wanyama Melabi Wambia, who died at Matulo Sub-location, on 11th March, 2007.
The Court will proceed to issue the same unless cause be shown to the contrary and appearance in this respect entered within thirty (30) days from the date of publication of this notice in the Kenya Gazette.
Dated the 6th May, 2014.
D. C. MUTAI,
                                                         Deputy Registrar, Bungoma.
