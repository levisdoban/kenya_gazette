REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Taiyo Mwita, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 4.3 hectares or thereabout, known as Bukira/Buhirimonono/1005, situate in the district of Kuria, and whereas the land registrar, Siaya, and whereas the said land has been subdivided, and whereas the land title deed issued earlier to Taiyo Mwita cannot be traced, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument, and upon such registration, the land title deed issued earlier to the said Taiyo Mwita, the shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
