GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 2487
THE VICTIM PROTECTION ACT
(No. 17 of 2014)
THE VICTIM PROTECTION BOARD
MEMBERSHIP
IT IS notified for the information of the general public that the following are the members of the Victim Protection Board under section 31(2) of the Victim Protection Act, 2014:
Under paragraph (b)—
the Director of the Witness Protection Agency who shall be the Secretary;
Under paragraph (c)—
Solicitor-General;
Under paragraph (d)—
the Principal Secretary of the Ministry for the time being responsible for correctional services;
Under paragraph (e)—
the Principal Secretary of the Ministry for the time being responsible for matters relating to children affairs;
Under paragraph (f)—
the Director of Public Prosecutions;
Under paragraph (g)—
the Inspector-General of Police or his or her representative;
Under paragraph (h)—
Simon Joni Ndubai;
Under paragraph (i)—
Christine Ochieng; and
Leah Owour.
Dated the 10th April, 2015.
GITHU MUIGAI,
Attorney-General.
