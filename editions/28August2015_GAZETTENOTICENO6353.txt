THE ENERGY REGULATORY COMMISSION
SCHEDULE OF TARIFFS 2013 FOR ELECTRICITY TARIFFS, PRICES, CHARGES AND RATES
WATER RESOURCE MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY LEVY
PURSUANT to clause 5 of Part III of   the  Schedule  of  Tariffs  2013, notice is given  that all  prices for  electrical energy specified in Part II -(A) of the said Schedule will be liable to a Water Resource Management Authority (WRMA) levy of  plus 5 cents per kWh. for  all meter readings  taken  in August, 2015.
Information used to calculate the WRMA Levy:
Approved WRMA levy for energy purchased from hydropower plants of capacity equal to or above 1MW = 5 Kenya cents per KWh.
