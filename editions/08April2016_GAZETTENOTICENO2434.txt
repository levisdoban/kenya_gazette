THE ADVOCATES ACT
THE COMPLAINTS COMMISSION
101ST QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT to section 53 (9) of the Advocates Act and rule 12 (1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st January, 2016 to 31st March, 2016, the Commission received a total of two hundred and eighty (280) new complaints out of which nineteen (19) were classified and files opened. Two hundred and sixty one (261) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
The nature of new complaints is as shown below:-

(a) Failure to account                                                       1
(b) Failure to keep client informed                                 Nil

(d) Withholding funds                                                    11
(e) Issuing Dishonored Cheques                                    Nil
(f)  Delay                                                                       Nil
(g) Others                                                                         1
Total                                                                            19

Preliminary Enquiry (PE) complaints
Total number of complaints subjected to Preliminary Enquiry (PE) was two hundred and sixty one (261).
