THE STATE CORPORATIONS  ACT
(Cap. 446)
THE KENYA ORDNANCE FACTORIES CORPORATION ORDER
(L.N. 125 of 1997)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) of the State Corporations Act, the Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Defence appoints—
E. Mutea Iringo
as a member of the Board of Directors of the Kenya Ordnance Factories Corporation Act (KOFC), for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 1st September, 2014. The appointment of Amb. Monica Juma (Dr.), is revoked.
Dated the 1st September, 2014.
RAYCHELLE OMAMO,
Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Defence.
