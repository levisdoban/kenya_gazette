APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by paragraph 5 (a) of the Kenya Accreditation Service Order, 2009, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces of the Republic of Kenya, appoint—
Robert Onsare Monda (Dr.)

President.
