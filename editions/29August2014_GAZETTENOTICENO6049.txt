REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS David Kiboi Ritho, of P.O. Box 491, Nyeri in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Laikipia/Daiga Umande Block 2/3819 (Akorino), situate in the district of Laikipia, and whereas the court in succession cause No. 861 of 2012 has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to (1) Florence Wanjiru Ritho and (2) Robert Githenya Ritho, both of P.O.
