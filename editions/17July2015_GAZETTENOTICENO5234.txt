98TH QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT To section 53 (9) of the Advocates Act and rule 12 (1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st April, 2015 to 30th June, 2015, the Commission received a total of two hundred and five (205) new complaints out of which thirty-seven (37) were classified and files opened. One hundred and sixty-eight (168) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
