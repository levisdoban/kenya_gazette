APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 11 (2) of the Central Bank of Kenya Act, I, Mwai Kibaki, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces appoint—
William Otiende Ogara (Dr.),
Mbui Wagacha (Dr.),
Vivienne Yeda Apopo (Ms.),
to be Non-Executive Directors of the Board of the Central Bank of Kenya, for a period of four (4) years, with effect from 14th March, 2011.
Dated the 28th March, 2011.
MWAI KIBAKI,
                President
