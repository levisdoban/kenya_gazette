THE ADVOCATES ACT
THE COMPLAINTS COMMISSION
96th Quarterly Report
PURSUANT to section 53(9) of the Advocates Act and Rule 12(1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st October, 2014 to 31st December, 2014, the commission received a total of one hundred and ninety-seven (197) new complaints out of which thrty-six (36) were classified and files opened. One hundred and sixty-one (161) were submitted for further enquiry under preliminary enquiry initiative.
The nature of new complaints is as shown below:

(a) Failure to account                                                10
(b) Failure to keep client informed                           Nil
(c) Failure to render professional services                  9
(d) Withholding funds                                               13
(e) Issuing Dishonored Cheques                                 2
(f) Delay                                                                   Nil
(g) Withholding documents                                         1
(h) Others                                                                   1
Total                                                                    36

Preliminary Enquiry (PE) complaints 
Total number of complaints subjected to Preliminary Enquiry (PE) was one hundred and sixty-one (161).
Total number of complaints settled under the Preliminary Enquiry (PE) was fifty-eight (58).
The matters referred to the Disciplinary Tribunal (DT) during the same period for further action and in accordance with section 57 (1) of the Advocates Act were three (3).
