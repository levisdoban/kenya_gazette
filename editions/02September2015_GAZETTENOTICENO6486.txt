THE NATIONAL POLICE SERVICE ACT
(No. 11A of 2011)
DECLARATION OF AN AREA AS DISTURBED OR DANGEROUS AREA
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 106 (1) of the National Police Service Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government, in consultation with the National Security Council, notifies the general public that Boni Forest covering the area from Holugho border point, Galmagala, Sangailu, Ijara, Bodhei, Milimani, Baurre, Basuba, Mangai, Maralani, Kiunga, Ishakani and Dar-es-Salam border point is declared a disturbed and dangerous area.
Dated the 2nd September, 2015.
JOSEPH NKAISSERY,
Cabinet Secretary for Interior
and Co-ordination of National Governmet.
