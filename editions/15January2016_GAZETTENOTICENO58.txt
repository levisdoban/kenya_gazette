THE KENYA MEDICAL TRAINING COLLEGE ACT
(Cap. 261)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 9 (1) (a) of the Kenya Medical Training College Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of Defence Forces, appoint—
Philip Kaloki (Prof.)
to be Chairperson of the Kenya Medical Training College Board of Management, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 15th Jaanuary, 2016.
Dated the 12th January, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
