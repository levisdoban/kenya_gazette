THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT CO-ORDINATION ACT
(No. 1 of 2013)
ESTABLISHMENT OF SUB-COUNTY SERVICE DELIVERY UNITS
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 14 (1) and (3) of the National Government Co-ordination Act, 2013, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government, with the approval of His Excellency Hon. Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, establishes the areas specified in the Schedule as Sub-county National Government Service Delivery Units for the purposes of the Act.
