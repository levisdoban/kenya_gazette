THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF BOMET
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to Standing Order 29 of the Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Bomet, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly of Bomet and the general public that the Assembly shall have a special sitting on Wednesday,  23rd  of  December, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. for the morning sitting  and at 2.30. p.m. for the afternoon sitting at the County Assembly Chambers in Bomet for the introduction and consideration of the Supplementary Appropriation Bill for the Financial Year 2015/16 and the debate on the motion for the impeachment of the Speaker.
Dated the 21st December, 2015.
G. K. KIPNGETICH,
Speaker.
