THE COPYRIGHT ACT
(No. 12 of 2001)
DECLARATION OF A COLLECTING SOCIETY
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 46 (2) of the Copyright Act, 2001, the Kenya Copyright Board declares Kopiken–the Reproduction Rights Society of Kenya to be a collecting society for literary authors and publishers, for a period of one year, with effect from 1st June, 2011.
Dated the 12th August, 2011.
MARISELLA OUMA,
                                                                   Executive Director,
Kenya Copyright Board.
HENRY CHAKAVA,
                                                                                     Chairman,
Kenya Copyright Board.
