REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Maria Nthuka Wambua alias Maria Thuka Mbua (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.809 hecatre or thereabouts, known as Kakuzi/Kirimiri Block 9/59 situate in the district of Thika, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Thika in succession cause No. 640 of 2011, has issued grant of letters of administration to Elizabeth Mbula Mule, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said Maria Nthuka Wambua alias Maria Thuka Mbua (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Maria Nthuka Wambua alias Maria Thuka Mbua (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
