IN THE SENIOR PRINCIPAL MAGISTRATE’S COURT
AT VIHIGA
PROBATE AND ADMINISTRATION
TAKE NOTICE that an application having been made in this court in:
Cause No. 59 of 2015
By Trufosa Jamoza Omedo, the deceased’s widow, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Julius Omedo alias Omedo Oracha, who died on 14th June, 2007.
The Court will proceed to issue the same unless cause be shown to the contrary and appearance in this respect entered within thirty (30) days from the date of publication of this notice in the Kenya Gazette.
Dated the 30th November, 2015.
S. N. MWANGI,
                                                         District Registrar, Vihiga.
