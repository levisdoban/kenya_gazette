THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Jacob Nkunja M’Ithara (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nyaki/Munithu/2023, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court in succession cause No. 176 of 2012, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of Damaris Mpinda Munyua, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the land title deed of the said piece of land is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in the name of Damaris Mpinda Munyua, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Jacob Nkunja M’Ithara (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 29th January, 2014.
B. K. KAMWARO,
MR/8406393                                   Land Registrar, Meru District.
