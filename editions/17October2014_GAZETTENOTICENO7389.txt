THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(NO. 3 OF 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Magiri Ntuara (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Kiirua/Ruiri/1051, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court of Kenya in succession cause No. 525 of 2004 has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of John Kirinya M'Magiri, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission RJL. 19 in respect of the said piece of land registered in the name of Magiri Ntuara (deceased), and whereas title deed issued issued in respect of the said piece of land has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission of RL. 19 in the name of John Kirinya M'Magiri, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Magiri Ntuara (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 17th October, 2014.
H. S. W. MUSUMIAH, MR/5834235                                    Land Registrar, Meru District.
