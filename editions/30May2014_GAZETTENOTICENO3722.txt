THE ELECTIONS (GENERAL) REGULATIONS, 2012
Voter Polling Stations for the By-Elections of Member of National Assembly- Bonchari Constituency and Members of County Assembly—Kinakomba, Misikhu and Maua Wards
Dated the 27th May, 2014. IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 86, 88, 101 (4), and 194 of the Constitution of Kenya, sections 16 and 19 of the Elections Act, 2011 and Regulations 7 and 8 of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission gives notice that voting for the Member of National Assembly- Bonchari Constituency: and Members of the Tana River, Bungoma and Meru County Assemblies- Kinakomba, Misikhu and Maua Wards respectively, By-elections scheduled for 23rd June, 2014 shall be conducted at the places gazetted vide Gazette Notice No. 1454 of 2013, published on 1st February, 2013.
A. I. HASSAN,
Chairperson,
Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission.
