IN THE MATTER OF AN APPLICATION FOR RELEASE OF LIQUIDATOR
Notice to Creditors and Contributories
 of Intention to Apply For Release
TAKE NOTICE that in accordance with section 247 (1) (3) of the Companies Act (Chapter 486) of the Laws of Kenya, as read together with Rule 152 of the Companies (Winding Up) Rules. We, the Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation formerly known as Deposit Protection Fund Board the Liquidator of the above named Company, Heritage Bank Limited (In Liquidation), intend to apply to the High Court for release of the liquidator.
And further take notice that any objection you may have to the granting of the release of the Liquidator must be notified to the High Court within twenty-one (21) days of the date hereof.
A summary of all receipts and payments in the winding up is ready for inspection at the Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation offices situate on 2nd Floor, CBK Pension House, Harambee Avenue, on any working day, from 9.00 a.m. to 3.00 p.m.
Dated the 11th September, 2014.
AGGREY JONATHAN BETT,
Acting Chief Executive Officer,
Kenya Deposit Insurance Corporation,
