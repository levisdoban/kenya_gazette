GAZETTE NOTICE NO.  473
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE ELECTIONS ACT
(No. 24 of 2011)
THE ELECTIONS (GENERAL) REGULATIONS, 2012
VOTER POLLING STATIONS—BY-ELECTION OF MASONGALENI AND NYANGORES WARD REPRESENTATIVES FOR MAKUENI AND BOMET COUNTY ASSEMBLIES, RESPECTIVELY
IN EXERCISE  of the powers conferred by Articles 86, 88 (4), 193, 194 (1) (a) of the Constitution of Kenya, section 19 of the Elections Act, 2011 and regulations 7 and 8 of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission gives notice that voting for the by-election for Masongaleni and Nyangores County Assembly Ward representatives for Makueni and Bomet County Assemblies, respectively, scheduled for 12th February, 2016, shall be conducted at the places listed in the Schedule to this notice.
And that:
(a)     The County Code to the County is listed in the First Column to the schedule.
(b)     The County Name is listed in the Second Column to the schedule.
(c)      The Constituency Code number is listed in the Third Column to the schedule.
(d)     The Constituency name is specified in the Fourth Column to the schedule.
(e)      The respective codes to the County Assembly Wards are listed in the Fifth Column to the schedule.
(f)       Every County Assembly Wards is listed in the Sixth Column to the schedule.
(g)     Each Constituency is respectively divided into Polling Centres and their Codes are listed in the Seventh Column to the schedule.
(h)     The Polling Stations are specified in the Eighth Column to the schedule and are each assigned with distinguishing numbers specified as Station Code in the Seventh Column to the schedule.
