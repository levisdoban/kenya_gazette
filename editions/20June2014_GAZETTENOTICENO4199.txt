THE POLITICAL PARTIES ACT
(NO. 11 OF 2011)
CHANGE OF PARTY CONSTITUTION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 20 (1) (A) of the Political Parties Act, 2011, the Registrar of Political Parties gives notice that United Democratic Forum intends to change or amend its constitution.
Any person with written submissions concerning the intended change by the political party shall within 7 days deposit them with the Registrar of Political Parties.
Further enquiries can be made through the Registrar’s offices, P.O. Box 45371–00100, Anniversary Towers University Way, and 21st Floor from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Dated the 9th April, 2014.
LUCY K. NDUNGU,
Registrar of Political Parties.
