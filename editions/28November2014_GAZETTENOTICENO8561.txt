THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
(Cap. 446)
NEW KENYA CO-OPERATIVE  CREAMERIES LIMITED
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (3) of the State Corporations Act, I Adan Mohamed, the Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Industrialization and Enterprise Development appoints—
Nixon Kipkemoi  Sigey
to be the Managing Director of the New Kenya Co-operative Creameries Limited, for a period of three (3) years with effect from 1st January, 2015. The appointment of Dr. Kipkirui Lang’at* is revoked with effect from 1st January, 2015.
Dated the 26th November, 2014.
ADAN MOHAMED,
Cabinet Secretary,
Ministry of Industrialization and Enterprise Development.
*G.N. 824 of 2014
