THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002, section 107)
PUBLIC CONSULTATION ON REGULAR TARIFF REVIEW FOR EMBU WATER AND SEWERAGE COMPANY LIMITED (EWASCO)
NOTICE is given to the general public in the area of Tana Water Services Board (TWSB), which has a license to provide water and sewerage services in the Counties of Murang’a, Embu, Kirinyaga, Nyeri, Meru and Tharaka Nithi that Tana Water Services Board has applied to the Water Services Regulatory Board for a regular tariff review for its agent, Embu Water and Sanitation Company, for the period 2014/2015 to 2016/2017. The Embu Water and Sanitation Company serves Embu municipality and its surrounding areas.
Tana Water Services Board proposes an upward tariff review to enable Embu Water and Sanitation Company to attain full cost recovery, undertake minor investments and meet conditions for improving service delivery.
Tana Water Services Board proposes to adopt rising block tariffs for all consumer categories except water kiosks.
Details of the current tariff and the proposed tariff can be obtained from www.tanawsb.or.ke or www.embuwater.co.ke or at Embu Water and Sanitation Company offices, situated off Muteero Road, next to Kenya National Library, Embu town.
Written comments on improvements on service delivery and/or justified objections to the proposed upward tariff review should be addressed to the Managing Director, Embu Water and Sanitation Company, P.O. Box 2142, Embu or info@embuwater.co.ke  or info@tanawsb.or.ke.
The closing date for such comments shall be on Friday 17th October, 2014.
A public consultation meeting shall be held on Tuesday 14th October, 2014 at Izaak Walton Inn starting at 9.00am. All members of the public within the service area of Embu Water and Sanitation Company are invited to attend.
Dated the 9th September, 2014.
