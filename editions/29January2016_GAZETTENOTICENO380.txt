THE NATIONAL TRANSPORT AND SAFETY AUTHORITY ACT
(No. 33 of 2012)
EXEMPTION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 26 (6) of the National Transport and Safety Authority Act, 2012, the Minister for Transport and Infrastructure exempts Postal Corporation of Kenya, from the Provisions of rule 5 (1) (a) of the National Transport and Safety Authority (Operations of Public Service Vehicles) Regulations, 2014, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 11th January, 2016.
Dated the 7th January, 2016.
JAMES W. MACHARIA,
Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure.
