THE NATIONAL POLICE SERVICE ACT (NO 11A OF 2011) DESIGNATION OF POLICE STATION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 40 (1) of the National Police Service Act, the Inspector-General designates—
Konoin Police Post
in Konoin District, (Map Reference No YQ28318), within Bomet County. to be a police station, with effect from 1st October. 2014.
Dated the 1st October, 2014.
DAVID M KIMAIYO,
Inspector-General, National Police Service.
