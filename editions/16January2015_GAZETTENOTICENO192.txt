THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE NAIROBI CITY COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
Special Sitting of the Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Orders 26 of the Nairobi City County Assembly Standing Orders, notice is given to all Members of the Nairobi City County Assembly that a Special Sitting of the County Assembly shall be held in the County Assembly Chamber, situated at Charter Hall, City Hall Way, on Tuesday 20th January, 2015 at 2.30 p.m.
At the sitting, the Nairobi City County Assembly will consider the Nairobi City County Urban Regularization of Developments Bill, 2015.
Dated the 13th January, 2015.
ALEX ole MAGELO,
MR/6742051           Speaker, Nairobi City County Assembly.
