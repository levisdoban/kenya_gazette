NOTICE is given pursuant to the provisions of the Disposal of Uncollected Goods (Cap. 38) of the Laws of Kenya, to the owners of DAF CF 85. 430 KBK 570 R, to take delivery of the said DAF CF 85. 430 KBK 570 R, which have  been lying at the premises of Opera (E.A.)
