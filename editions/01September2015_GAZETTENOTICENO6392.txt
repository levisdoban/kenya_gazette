THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE MURANG’A COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE ASSEMBLY
NOTICE is given to all Members of the County Assembly that pursuant to Standing Order 30 (3) of the Murang’a County Assembly Standing Orders, on the request of the Assembly Majority Leader, with the support of the requisite number of Members of the County Assembly, I have appointed Wednesday, 2nd September, 2015 and Monday, 7th September, 2015 as a day for a special sitting of the County Assembly. The sitting shall be held at the Murang’a County Assembly Chamber, Murang’a at 10.00 a.m. on each respective day. The business to be transacted at the sitting shall be to consider the removal of George Kamau, County Executive Committee Member in charge of Finance, IT and Planning, Murang’a County.
In accordance with Standing Order 30 (4) of the Murang’a County Assembly Standing Orders, the business specified in this Notice shall be the only business before the County Assembly during the special sittings, following which the County Assembly shall stand adjourned until Tuesday, 15th September, 2015 at 9.30 a.m. in accordance with the resolution of the County Assembly made on Thursday, 5th February, 2015.
Dated the 31st August, 2015.
LEONARD NDUATI KARIUKI,
Speaker, Murang’a County Assembly.
