THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF KIRINYAGA STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
NOTICE is given to all Members of the County Assembly of Kirinyaga and the general public that pursuant to Standing Order 26 (3) of the Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Kirinyaga, that a special sitting of the County Assembly shall be held at the County Assembly Chamber on Thursday, 3rd December, 2015 at 6.35 p.m for purposes of—
(a) third reading of the Kirinyaga County Supplementary Appropriation Bill, 2015; and
(b)    third reading of the Kirinyaga County Finance Bill, 2015.
Dated the 30th of November, 2015.
ANNE WANGECHI GITHINJI,
Speaker of the County Assembly of Kirinyaga.
