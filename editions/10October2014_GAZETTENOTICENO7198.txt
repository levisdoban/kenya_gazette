KARURI WATER AND SANITATION COMPANY LIMITED
Regular Tariff Adjustment
THE Water Services Regulatory Board pursuant to section 73 (5) of the Water Act, 2002, approved a Regular Tariff increase for water services for Karuri Water and Sanitation Company Limited.
Under the powers granted under section 73 (1) and (4) of the Water Act, 2002, and section 120 (1) of County Governments Act, 2012, Athi Water Services Board in conjunction with the County Government of Kiambu do hereby give a notice to all existing and potential water customers of Karuri Water and Sanitation Company Limited of the new adjusted water tariffs effective from 1st November, 2014 and will be reviewed on 1st October, 2017.
