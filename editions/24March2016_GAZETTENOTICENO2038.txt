THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
SPECIAL SITTING OF PARLIAMENT
NOTICE is given to all Members of the National Assembly that, pursuant to the provisions of Article 132 (1) (c) (i) and (iii) of the Constitution of Kenya, His Excellency Hon. Uhuru Kenyatta, C.G.H., President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, shall address a Special Sitting of Parliament on Thursday, 31st March, 2016, at 2.30 p.m.
In accordance with the provisions of the Standing Order 22 of the National Assembly Standing Orders, all Members of the National Assembly are notified that a Special Sitting of Parliament shall be held on Thursday, 31st March, 2016, at 2.30 p.m. at the National Assembly Chamber, Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi.
Dated the 22nd March, 2016.
JUSTIN B. N. MUTURI,
Speaker of the National Assembly.
