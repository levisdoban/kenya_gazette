
				Page: 1792GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 4882
THE WILDLIFE (CONSERVATION AND MANAGEMENT ACT
(NO. 47 OF 2013)
INTENT TO DECLARE A WILDLIFE SANCTUARY
PURSUANT to Article 10 (2) (a) of the Constitution and the Fourth Schedule to the Wildlife (Conservation and Management) Act, 2013, notice is given of the intention to gazette all that area of land measuring approximately 217 hectares situate north of of Kiboko Trading Centre and East Kiboko Ranch within Makueni County, the boundaries of which are particularly delineated, edged purple, on  Boundary Plan No. 216/66 which is signed, sealed with the seal of the Survey of Kenya, and deposited at the survey records office, Survey of Kenya, Nairobi, as Wildlife Sanctuary.
Any interested persons who wish to make presentation or objection to the intended declaration of the Wildlife Sanctary may send such presentation or objection, in writing, to the Acting Director-General, Kenya Wildlife Service, P.O. Box 40241–00100, Nairobi, not later than sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this Gazette notice and any such presentation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
A copy of the Boundary Plan has been deposited for public inspection at the office of the Acting Director-General, Kenya Wildlife Service, Nairobi. The copies of the Boundary plan deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the above mentioned address between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m.
Dated the 4th July, 2014.
JUDI WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary for Environment,
Water and Natural Resources.
