APPOINTMENT OF CONSTITUENCY UWEZO FUND MANAGEMENT COMMITTEES
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Section 15 (5) of the Public Finance Management (Uwezo Fund) Regulations, 2014, the Cabinet Secretary Ministry of Devolution and Planning gazettes the following members of Constituency Uwezo Fund Committees in various constituencies as outlined below for a period of 3 years with effect from 30th October, 2014
LIMURU CONSTITUENCY
Charlene Mukuhi Waweru replacing Alice Wanjiku Ndungu and Edward Karega Muchiri replacing Peter Muchendu to the Limuru Constituency Uwezo Fund Management Committee.
The appointment of Alice Wanjiku Ndungu, Peter Muchendu vide Gazette Notice 1718 of 2014, is revoked.
WUNDANYI CONSTITUENCY
Joseph Mwawas replacing Manuel S. Kirigha and Joram L. Walonyo replacing Evalyne C. Kogetgei to the Wundanyi Constituency Uwezo Fund Management Committee
The appointment of Manuel S. Kirigha, Evalyne C. Kogetgei vide Gazette Notice 1718 of 2014, is revoked.
WAJIR NORTH CONSTITUENCY
Said Osman Ibrahim replacing Mohammed Abdullahi Abalye to Wajir North Constituency Uwezo Fund Management Committee
The appointment of Mohammed Abdullahi Abalye vide Gazette Notice 1718 of 2014, is revoked.
GATUNDU NORTH CONSTITUENCY
Mary Wangui Wahogo replacing Joseph Ngamau Wamburu to the Gatundu North Constituency Uwezo Fund Management Committee
The appointment of Joseph Ngamau Wamburu as vide Gazette Notice 1926 of 2014, is revoked.
ENDEBESS CONSTITUENCY
Patrick Kaboi Bulunya replacing Philip Naibei Sakong to the Endebess Constituency Uwezo Fund Management Committee
The appointment of Philip Naibei Sakong vide Gazette Notice 1926 of 2014, is revoked.
Dated the 29th April, 2015.
ANNE WAIGURU,
Cabinet Secretary for Devolution and Planning.
