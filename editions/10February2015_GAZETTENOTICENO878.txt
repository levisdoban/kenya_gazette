GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 878
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF KISII
REGULAR SESSION OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF KISII
PURSUANT to standing order 24 (1) of the County Assembly Standing Orders, 2013, the County Assembly of Kisii re-opens its regular sessions on Tuesday the 10th February, 2015 at 2.30 p.m.
The purpose of this notice is to notify the County Assembly Members and the general public on the intended re-opening.
KEROSI ONDIEKI,
Speaker, Kisii County Assembly.
