REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Gathuna Kabui (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Dagoretti/Waithaka/108, situate in the district of Nairobi, and whereas the High Court at Nairobi in succession cause No. 927 of 2002, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of (1) Beth Nyokabi Gathuna, (2) Mary Nyokabi Mwangi and (3) Peter Wambu Gathuna, all of P.O. Box 846, Nairobi, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in respect of the said piece of land, and whereas the land title deed is lost, notice is hereby given that after the expiration of sixty (60) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in favour of (1) Beth Nyokabi Gathuna, (2) Mary Nyokabi Mwangi and (3) Peter Wambu Gathuna, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Gathuna Kabui (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
