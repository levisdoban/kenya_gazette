
				Page: 999GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 1732
THE SPORTS ACT
(No. 25 of 2013)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 35 (1) (a) of the Sports Act, 2013, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Paul Tergat
to be the Chairperson of the Kenya Academy of Sports Council, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 18th March, 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
