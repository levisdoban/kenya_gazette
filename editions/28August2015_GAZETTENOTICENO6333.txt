THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Edward Muthi (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.88 acre or thereabouts, known as Kabete/Lower Kabete/42, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Kiambu in succession cause No. 7 of 2013, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) John Gichuru Muuthi, (2) Horridah Waithira Muuthi and (3) Lawis Kiarie Muuthi, and whereas the said title deed issued to the said Edward Muthi (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and issue a land title deed to the said (1) John Gichuru Muuthi, (2) Horridah Waithira Muuthi and (3) Lawis Kiarie Muuthi, and upon such registration the land title deed issued to Edward Muthi (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 28th August, 2015.
I. N. NJIRU,
MR/8055157                               Land Registrar, Kiambu District.
