THE KENYA WATER INSTITUTE ACT
(No. 11 of 2001)
KENYA WATER INSTITUTE
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (1) (a) of the KEWI Act, 2001, the Cabinet Secretary for Water and Irrigation appoints—
Benjamin Muema
to be Chairman of the Governing Council of Kenya Water Institute, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 22nd  January, 2016 and revokes the appointment of Amb. Mary M. Khimulu.
Dated the 20th January, 2016.
EUGENE LUDOVIC WAMALWA,
Cabinet Secretary for Water and Irigation.
