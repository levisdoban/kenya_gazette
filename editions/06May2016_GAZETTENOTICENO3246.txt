GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 3246
THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Muruga Mbogo (deceased), of Kirinyaga in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Inoi/Kimandi/202, situate in the district of Kirinyaga and whereas the High Court at Kerugoya in succession cause No. 430 of 2015, has issued grant and confirmation letters to Judith Kariuko Muruga, and whereas all efforts made to recover the land title deed be surrendered to the land registrar for cancellation have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said administration letters to Judith Kariuko Muruga, and upon such registration, the land title deed issued earlier to the said Muruga Mbogo (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 6th May, 2016.
J. K. MUTHEE,
MR/8220933                           Land Registrar, Kirinyaga District.
