REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS George Thandi Thiong’o (deceased), is registered as proprietor of those pieces of land known as Dagoretti/Uthiru/982, 984, 986 containing 0.046, 0.36, 0.27 hectare or thereabouts, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the High Court of Kenya at Nairobi in succession cause No. 993 of 1999, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) James Thiong’o Kaniaru, (2) Damaris Nduta Kaniaru, (3) Ashton Ngugi Kaniaru and (4) Samuel Njoroge Kaniaru, and whereas the said land title deeds issued earlier to the said George Thandi Thiong’o (deceased) have been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period.
