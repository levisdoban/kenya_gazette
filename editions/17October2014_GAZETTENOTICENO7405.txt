95TH QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT to section 53 (9) of the Advocates Act and Rule 12 (1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st July, 2014, to 30th September, 2014, the Commission received a total of two hundred and eight (208) new complaints out of which twenty (20) were classified and files opened. One hundred and eighty eight (188) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
