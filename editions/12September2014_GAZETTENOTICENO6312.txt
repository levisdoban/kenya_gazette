SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF THARAKA NITHI
PURSUANT to Standing Order 26 (1)–(3) of the Standing Orders of the County Assembly, the Speaker of the County Assembly of Tharaka Nithi on request by both Majority and Minority leaders informs the members of the County Assembly of Tharaka Nithi, that a special sitting of the Assembly shall be held on Wedneday, 3rd September, 2014 in the Assembly Chambers, at 9.00 a.m. The agenda will be consideration of appoved Bills, 2014.
