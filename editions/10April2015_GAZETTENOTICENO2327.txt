THE PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES ACT
(No. 14 of 2003)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 4 (1) (c) (ii) of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 2003, the Cabinet Secretary for Labour, Social Security and Services appoints—
Anastacia Cecilia Magoma Odhiambo
to be a member of the National Council for Persons with Disabilities for a period of three (3) years. The appointment* of Mary Mwangi Kabaru is revoked.
Dated the 26th March, 2015.
SAMWEL KAZUNGU KAMBI,
Cabinet Secretary for  Labour, Social Security and Services.
*G.N. 3230/2014.
