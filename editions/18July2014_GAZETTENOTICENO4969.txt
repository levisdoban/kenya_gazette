THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Daniel Wangewa (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.83 acre or thereabouts, known as Dagoretti/Riruta/51, situate in the district of Nairobi, and whereas the High Court at Nairobi in succession cause No. 1293 of 2002 has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) Robert Githinji Wangewa, (2) Patrick Kibe Wangewa and (3) Peter Kamuyu Wangewa, and whereas the land title deed issued earlier to Daniel Wangewa (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and issue a land title deed to the said (1) Robert Githinji Wangewa, (2) Patrick Kibe Wangewa and (3) Peter Kamuyu Wangewa, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Daniel Wangewa (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
