THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
 (No. 6 of 2012)
APPOINTMENT
Establishment of Marsabit County Registration Unit
PURSUANT to section 6 (1), (2), (3), (4), (5) and (6) of the Land Registration Act and further to consultations with the Marsabit County Government, The National Land Commission constitutes a Land Registration Unit in Marsabit County.
The Registration Unit will serve Marsabit County and becomes operational from the date of publication of this notice hereof.
Dated the 16th April, 2015.
MUHAMMAD A. S. WAZURI,
MR/7407294                     Chairman, National Land Commission.
