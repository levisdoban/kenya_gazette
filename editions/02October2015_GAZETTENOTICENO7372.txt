THE SENATE STANDING ORDERS
JOINT SITTING OF PARLIAMENT
NOTICE is given to all Senators that pursuant to Standing Order 25 (2) of the Senate Standing Orders, the Speaker of the Senate, after consultation with the Speaker of the National Assembly, has arranged for a Joint Sitting of Parliament to be held on Tuesday, 6th October, 2015 at 2:30 p.m. for purposes of an address by His Excellency, Jakaya M. Kikwete (Dr.), President of the United Republic of Tanzania.
Accordingly, I notify all Senators that the Joint Sitting of Parliament shall be held on Tuesday, 6th October, 2015 commencing at 2:30 p.m. at the National Assembly Chamber, Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi.
Dated the 1st October, 2015.
EKWEE ETHURO,
Speaker of the Senate.
