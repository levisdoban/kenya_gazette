THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF BOMET STANDING ORDERS
Special Sitting of the County Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Order 28 of the County Assembly of Bomet, it is notified for the information of the members of the County Assembly of Bomet and the general public that the Assembly shall have a Special Sitting on Monday, 26th January, 2015 at 2.30 p.m. for the tabling of a nominee to the Bomet Land Management Board from Konoin Sub-County and on Thurday, 29th January, 2015 at 9.00 a.m. at the County Assembly Chamber in Bomet for consideration of the list of nominees to the Bomet Land Management Board.
Dated the 22nd of January, 2015.
G. K. KIPNGETICH,
MR/6742326                Speaker of the County Assembly of Bomet.
