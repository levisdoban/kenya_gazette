THE SIAYA COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to Standing Order No. 27 (3) of the County Assembly of Siaya Standing Orders and following a request by the leader of Majority Party in consultation with the Committee on Budget and Appropriations, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly and the general public that there will be a special sitting of the County Assembly which shall be held in the County Assembly Chambers, in Siaya on Thursday, 17th December, 2015 at 3.30 pm for purposes of: Consideration and Approval of Supplementary Budget for FY2015/2016.
Dated the 15th December, 2015.
GEORGE O. OKODE,
Speaker, County Assembly of Siaya.
