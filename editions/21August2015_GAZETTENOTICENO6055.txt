THE INDUSTRIAL PROPERTY ACT
(No. 3 of 2001)
THE INDUSTRIAL PROPERTY TRIBUNAL
Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 113 (1) of the Industrial Property Act, 2001, the Cabinet Secretary for Industrialization and Enterprise Development appoints—
Hassan N. Lakicha
to be Chairman of the Industrial Property Tribunal for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 11th August, 2015.
Dated the 11th August, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
                  Cabinet Secretary for Industrialization and Enterprise Development.
