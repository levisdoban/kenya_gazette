THE ADVOCATES ACT
THE COMPLAINTS COMMISSION
94TH QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT to Section 53(9) of the Advocates Act and Rule 12(1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st April 2014 to 30th June 2014, the Commission received a total of two hundred and eighteen (218) new complaints out of which twenty seven (27) were classified and files opened. One hundred and ninety one (191) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
