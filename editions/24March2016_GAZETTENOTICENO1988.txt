REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Twaruchiu Muraa (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Kiirua/Ruiri/1004, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court of Kenya at Meru in
succession cause No. 486 of 2011, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant to (1) Dadalina Wambui Riungu and (2) David Kirimi Riungu, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said Twaruchiu Muraa (deceased), has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 in the name of (1) Dadalina Wambui Riungu and (2) David Kirimi Riungu, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Twaruchiu Muraa (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
