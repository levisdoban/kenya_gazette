COMPLETION OF PART DEVELOPMENT PLANS
(PDP No. MS/350/2014/01 for Existing Site for Chuka Family Life Centre; PDP No. MS/350/2014/02 for Existing Site for Chuka/Igambang’ombe C.D.F. Officers and PDP No. KTN/2563/2014/01 for Kathwana Local Physical Development Plan (Zoning)).
NOTICE is given that the above-mentioned development plans were completed on 5th May, 2014, 5th May, 2014 and 30th January, 2014, respectively.
The development plans relate to land situated within different parts of Tharaka Nithi County.
Copies of the part development plans have been deposited for public inspection at the County Physical Planning Office, Chuka.
The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at offices of the County Government in Kathwana, County Commissioner, Chuka and County Physical Planning Officer, Chuka between the hours of 7.30 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objection to the above-named part development plan may send such representations or objections in writing to be received by the County Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 347–60400, Chuka, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
