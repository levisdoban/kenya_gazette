(No. 28 of 2013)
THE NATIONAL RESEARCH FUND
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 32 (4) (a) of the Science, Technology and Innovation Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Education, Science and Technology appoints—
Tirop Kosgey
to be the Chairman of the National Research Fund, for  a period of three (3) years, with effect from 5th October, 2015.
Dated the 26th October, 2015.
JACOB T. KAIMENYI,
Cabinet Secretary for Education,
Science and Technology.
