THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE ELECTIONS ACT
(NO. 24 OF 2011)
THE ELECTION (GENERAL) REGULATIONS, 2012
Parliamentary By-Elections:  Gatundu South and Mathare Constituencies and County Assembly Representative for Kibiri Ward.
(a) Vacancies occurred in the offices of Members of the National Assembly Gatundu South Constituency, Mathare Constituency and the office of the County Assembly Ward Representative, Kibiri Ward on 21st May, 2014, 26th May, 2014 and 11th April, 2014 respectively.IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 84, 85, 88 (4), 101 (4) and 194 (1) (a) of the Constitution of Kenya, sections, 13(3), 16, 19, 22, 24, 25, 26, 29, 32, 33, 38, 74 of the Elections Act, and Regulations 11 (4)-(5) and 12 (1), PART III,V and VIII of the Elections (General) Regulations, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission gives notice that:
(b) Independent Candidates to submit their symbols on or before 19th June, 2014 being 21 days before the Commission nomination days.
(c)  Each Political Party wishing to participate in the elections must finalize the nominations and determine intra-party disputes arising from party nomination of its candidate(s) on or before the 23rd June, 2014.
(d) Submission of the list and details of the Political Party nominated candidates and the Independent candidates to the Commission shall be on 5th July, 2014.
(e)  Disputes relating to or arising from nominations shall be determined within seven (7) days of lodging the dispute(s) with the Commission.
(f)   The days for the nomination of Political Parties candidates and Independent candidates for the By-Election will be on Thursday, 10th July, 2014 and Friday, 11th July, 2014, and nomination papers shall be delivered by candidates to the Returning Officers between the hours of eight o’clock in the morning and four o’clock in the afternoon.
(g) Disputes relating to or arising from decisions of the Returning Officers after nominations shall be determined within seven days of lodging the dispute(s) with the Commission.
(h) Pursuant to the provisions of Regulation 57 (1) and (2) of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012 every Political Party and every Independent Candidate at the by-election shall submit to the Commission names of the Chief Agents on or before 23rd July, 2014.
(i)   The campaign period for purposes of the 7th August, 2014 By-Elections shall commence on Thursday, 10th July, 2014 and cease on Tuesday, 5th August, 2014 being 24 hours before the By- Election Day which is 7th August, 2014.
(j)   The campaign time shall run from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. during the campaign period.
(k)  If the by-elections are contested, the poll will take place on the 7th August, 2014.
Notes:
The attention of candidates and persons subscribing nomination papers is drawn to the provisions of Part III, V, and VIII of the Election (General) Regulations on the Parliamentary Elections (Subsidiary Legislation), First Schedule to the Leadership and Integrity Act No. 19 of 2012 and Chapter Six of the Constitution of Kenya.
Every political party and every person who participates in the by- election shall subscribe to and observe the Electoral Code of Conduct set out in the Second Schedule to the Elections Act, 2011.
In pursuance to the provisions of section 26 of the Elections Act, 2011 any candidate who intends to vie for the by-election should not participate in public fundraising.
Dated the 13th June, 2014.
A. I. HASSAN,
Chairperson,
Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission
