GAZETTE NOTICE NO.  8567
THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS M’Muguongo M’Angecia Charles (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 3.8 hectares or thereabout, known as Mikinduri/Athwana/501, situate in the district of Meru North, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Maua in succession cause No. 65 of 2010, has issued grant of letters of administration to Rita Mwathwana Baariu, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said M’Muguongo M’Angecia Charles (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said M’Muguongo M’Angecia Charles (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 13th November, 2015.
D. M. KAMANJA,
MR/8477426                         Land Registrar, Meru North District.
