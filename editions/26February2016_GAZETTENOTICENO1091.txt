THE WEIGHTS AND MEASURES ACT
(Cap. 513)
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENT OF KAJIADO
WEIGHTS AND MEASURES DIRECTORATE
STAMPING STATIONS FOR VERIFICATION OF WEIGHTS, MEASURES,WEIGHING AND MEASURING EQUIPMENT USED FOR TRADE
PURSUANT to the powers conferred to the Governor and in execution of the functions of the Weights and Measures Directorate under section 27 of the Weights and Measures Act, Cap. 513, Laws of Kenya, the County Executive Committee Member in-charge of Trade, Co-operatives, Enterprise Development and Tourism, Kajiado County, gazettes the following centers as stamping stations and their respective dates for the annual verification and stamping exercise.
Where the equipment is bulky, delicate or by reason of its being permanently fixed, it shall be sufficient for the purpose of this section if the person who has the equipment notifies in writing its position to the inspector or some other officer authorized by the inspector to receive the notification at least (3) days in advance of the date the verification falls due.
