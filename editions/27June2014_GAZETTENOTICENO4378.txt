THE PUBLIC HEALTH ACT (CAP. 242)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 9 of the Public Health Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Health appoints—
NICHOLAS MWANGI MURAGURI (DR.)
to be the Director of Medical Services, for a period of five years, with effect from the 1st July, 2014 and revokes the appointment* of Francis Mwihia Kimani (Dr.).
Dated the 26th June, 2014.
JAMES MACHARIA, Cabinet Secretary for Ministry of Health. 
*G.N. 3979/2008.
