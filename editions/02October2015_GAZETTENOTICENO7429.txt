THE KENYA MARITIME AUTHORITY ACT
(Cap. 370)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (d) of the Kenya Maritime Authority Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure appoints—
Malika Omar
to be a member of the Board of Kenya Maritime Authority, for a period of three (3)  years, with effect from 2nd October, 2015. 
Dated the 2nd October, 2015.
JAMES MACHARIA,
Ag. Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure.
