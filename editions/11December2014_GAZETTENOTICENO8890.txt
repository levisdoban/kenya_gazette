TASKFORCE TO DEVELOP BAIL AND BOND POLICY GUIDELINES
Extension of Time Frame
TAKE NOTICE that the time frame for the Task Force on the Development of Bail and Bond appointed on 30th May, 2014, pursuant to Article 49 (1) (h) and sub-article (2) of the Constitution of Kenya, 2010, the provisions of the Criminal Procedure Code Cap. 75 Laws of Kenya related to the application of bail and bond, and section 34 of the Judicial Service Act, to develop a Bail Policy Guidelines aimed at streamlining and addressing disparities in the application of bail and bond for arrested and accused persons has been extended to 30th March, 2015.
