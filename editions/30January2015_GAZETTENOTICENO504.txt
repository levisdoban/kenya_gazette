THE WATER ACT
(Cap. 372)
Revocation of Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 46 (3) (a) of the Water Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, revokes the *appointment of—
Patricia Kameri Mbote (Prof.)
as Chairman of the Water Services Regulatory Board, with effect from 2nd February, 2015.
Dated the 28th January, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
*G.N. 14700/2012.
