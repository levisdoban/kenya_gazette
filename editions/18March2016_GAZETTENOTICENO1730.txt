
				Page: 999GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 1730
THE ENERGY ACT
(Cap. 314)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 10 (1) (a) of the Energy Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Joshua Nyamweya Oigara
to be the Chairperson of the Energy Regulatory Commission, for a period of four (4) years,  with effect from the 18th March, 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
