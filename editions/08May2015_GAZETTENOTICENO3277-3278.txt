RYCE EAST AFRICA LIMITED
Disposal of Uncollected Goods
NOTICE is issued pursuant to the provisions of the Disposal of Uncollected Goods Act (Cap. 38) of the laws of Kenya, to the owners namely Messrs. Telkom Kenya Limited of motor vehicles reg. Nos. KAD 187M, Daihatsu Feroza and KAL 176 U DCM Toyota, Olive Oritchlow, of motor vehicle No. KWF 374 and unidentified owner of motor vehicle No. KWF 374 and unidentified owner of motor vehicle No. KAB 659T, which are lying at the premises of Messrs. Ryce East Africa Limited, Kampala Road, Nairobi, to take delivery of the said motor vehicle within thirty (30) days from the date of publication of this notice upon payment of all outstanding debt balances, storage, security and any other incidental costs incurred as at the expiry of the notice including the costs of publication of this notice.
Failure of which the said motor vehicles shall be sold either by public auction, tender or private treaty and the proceeds of the sale be defrayed against all accrued charges without any further reference to the owners.
SHEBA MOHAMED,
