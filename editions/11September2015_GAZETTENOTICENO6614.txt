THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY (POWERS AND
PRIVILEDGES) ACT
(Cap. 6)
Declaration of the Nairobi City County Assembly Precincts
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 117 (2) and 186 of the Constitution as read together with section 17 (2) of the County Governments Act, 2012, sections 2, 8 and 13 of the National Assembly (Powers and Privileges) Act Chapter 6, Standing Orders Nos. 224, 225 and 226 of the Nairobi City County Assembly Standing Orders,  the Speaker of the Nairobi City County Assembly declares the precincts of the Nairobi City County Assembly to be the Northern Wing of the City Hall Building and shall include:
(a) The Chamber (formerly conference hall), every part of the building in which the chamber is situated and the galleries therein.
(b) The offices of the Assembly, from the basement parking to the 5th floor of the Northern Wing City hall building and any other office space therein.
(c) The places within the building provided for the use and accommodation of members, strangers, members of the public and representatives of the press.
(d) The parking space reserved for the use of the Assembly that is located in front of the Supreme Court Building along Taifa Road.
And any forecourt, yard, garden, enclosure or open space appurtenant thereto and used or provided for the purposes of the Assembly.
“Stranger” in relation to the Assembly means any person other than the speaker, a member of the assembly or an officer of the assembly.
The Speaker or any other authorized officer may regulate the admittance of strangers within the said precincts.
Dated the 8th September, 2015.
ALEX OLE MAGELO,
MR/8045885                  Speaker, Nairobi City County Assembly.
