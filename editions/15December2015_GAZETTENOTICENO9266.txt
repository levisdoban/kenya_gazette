THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE SENATE
THE SENATE STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE SENATE
NOTICE is given to all Senators that pursuant to standing order 29 of the Senate Standing Orders, on the request of the Senate Majority Leader supported by fifteen (15) Senators, I have appointed Wednesday, 16th December, 2015 as a day for a special sitting of the Senate. The special sitting shall be held in the Senate Chamber, Ground Floor, Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi, at 3.00 p.m.
The business to be transacted at the special sitting shall be the consideration of—
The Presidential Memorandum on the Public Procurement and Asset Disposal Bill (National Assembly Bill No. 40 of 2014);
The Presidential Memorandum on the Public Audit Bill (National Assembly Bill No. 38 of 2014); and
The Water Bill, (National Assembly Bill, No. 7 of 2014).
In accordance with standing order 29(5) of the Senate Standing Orders, the business specified in this Notice shall be the only business before the Senate during the Special Sitting, following which the Senate shall stand adjourned until Tuesday, 9th February, 2016, at 2:30 p.m. in accordance with the Resolution of the Senate made on Thursday, 3rd  December, 2015.
Dated the 15th December, 2015.
EKWEE ETHURO,
Speaker of the Senate.
