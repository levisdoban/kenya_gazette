THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Paul Omondi Ajuang alias Paul Omondi Ajwang (deceased), of Siaya in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of land known as Siaya/Nyandiwa/773, situate in the district of Siaya, and whereas the High Court of Kenya at Kisumu in succession cause No. H.C./R.M. 520 of 2015, has ordered that the said piece of land be registered in the name of Peter Odhiambo Omondi, and whereas efforts made to recover the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land by the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said grant document and issue a land title deed to the said Peter Odhiambo Omondi, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Paul Omondi Ajuang alias Paul Omondi Ajwang (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 16th October, 2015.
P. A. OWEYA,
MR/8220966                                   Land Registrar, Siaya District.
