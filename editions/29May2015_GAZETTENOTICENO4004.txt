THE KENYA AGRICULTURAL AND LIVESTOCK RESEARCH ACT
(No. 17 of 2013)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) of the Kenya Agricultural and Livestock Research Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries appoints—
Under subsection (b–g)—
Principal Secretary, State Department of Agriculture;
Principal Secretary, National Treasury;
Principal Secretary, Ministry of Industrialization and Enterprise Development;
Principal Secretary, State Department for Environment;
Principal Secretary, State Department of Livestock;
Secretary, National Council for Science and Technology;
Under subsection (i) (ii)—
Mabel Imbuga (Prof.),
Under subsection (i) (iii)—
Kassim O. Farah (Prof.),
as members of the Board of Directors of Kenya Agricultural and Livestock Research Organization, with effect from the 2nd June, 2015.
Dated the 28th May, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
Ag. Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries.
