REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Phyllis Wangui w/o David Waiyaki Munyua (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.092 hectare or thereabouts, known as Dagoretti/Thogoto/T. 195, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Kiambu in succession cause No. 124 of 2005, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) Charles Gitau Waiyaki and (2) Simon Ngugi Waiyaki, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said Phyllis Wangui w/o David Waiyaki Munyua (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Phyllis Wangui w/o David Waiyaki Munyua (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
