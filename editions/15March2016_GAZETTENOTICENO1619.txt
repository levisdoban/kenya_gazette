THE FIREARMS ACT
(Cap. 114)
APPOINTMENT OF THE FIREARMS LICENSING BOARD
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (1) (2) and (3) of The Firearms Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government appoints the following to be members of the Firearms Licensing Board:
Maj. Gen. (Rtd.) Enoch M. Sasia—(Chairman);
Members:
Lilian Kiamba (Ms.).
Stanley Omucheyi.
Bernice Gachegu (Ms.).
Anthony Wahome.
William Sing’oei.
George K. Waiguru.
James I. Ngului.
Samuel C. Kimaru—(Secretary).
The members of the Board will serve for a period of three (3) years with effect from 16th March, 2016.
JOSEPH NKAISSERRY,
Cabinet Secretary for Interior
and Co-ordination of National Government.
