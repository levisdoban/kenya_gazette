THE CONSTITUENCIES DEVELOPMENT FUND ACT
(No. 30 of 2013)
Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 24 (4) of the Constituencies Develoment Fund Act, 2013, the Cabinet Secretary for Devolution and Planning appoints—
Edward Thirikwa
to be a member of the Tetu Constituency Development Committee, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 2nd March, 2015. The Gazettement of Jane Wairimu Githinji*, is revoked.
Dated the 4th March, 2015.
ANNE WAIGURU,
