THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE ETHICS AND ANTI-CORRUPTION COMMISSION ACT, 2011
(No. 22 of 2011)
Appointment of Chairperson and Members of the Ethics And Anti-Corruption Commission
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred on me by Article 250 (2) (c) of the Constitution and section 10 (5) of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission Act, 2011, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, appoint—
Philip K.B. Kinisu—(Chairperson)
Dabar Maalim Abdi (Dr.)
Paul Mwaniki Gachoka
Sophia Lepuchirit
Rose Mghoi Mtambo Macharia
to be the chairperson and members of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission  respectively, for a period  of six years, with effect from the 17th December, 2015.
Dated the 17th December, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
