THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF ISIOLO
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to the provisions of the Standing Order 26 of the Interim Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Isiolo, it is notified for the information of the Members of the County Assembly that a special sitting shall be held in the County Assembly chamber in Isiolo, on Friday, 15th May, 2015 at 2.30 p.m. to deliberate on the on-going serious conflict between communities in Kinna Ward and the Kenya Wildlife Service (KWS).
Dated the 13th May, 2015.
MOHAMED TUBI BIDU,
                                              Speaker, County Assembly of Isiolo.
