THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
(Cap. 446)
THE KENYA LEATHER DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL ORDER, 2011
RE-APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) (a) of the State Corporations Act and paragraph 4 (1) (a) of the Kenya Leather Development Council Order, 2011, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, re-appoint—
Titus Ibui
to be the Chairman of the Kenya Leather Development Council Board, up to 25th  October, 2017, with effect from the 26th October, 2014.
Dated the 21st November, 2014.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
