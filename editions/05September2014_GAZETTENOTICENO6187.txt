THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT (No. 3 of 2012)REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Edward Njuguna Muranga (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nyandarua/Njabini/200, situate in the district of Nyandarua, and whereas the High Court at Nakuru in succession cause No. 764 of 2012, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) Peter Kamau Njuguna and (2) Njuku Njuguna Muranga, and whereas the land title deed issued earlier to the said Edward Njuguna Muranga (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Edward Njuguna Muranga (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 5th September, 2014.
J. W. KARANJA, MR/5748606                         Land Registrar, Nyandarua!Samburu Districts.
