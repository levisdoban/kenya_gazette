THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(Cap. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
(Extension Order)
WHEREAS by an extension order dated 30th March, 2015, I appointed Peter Wanjohi Kiama, Chief Co-operative Officer, P.O. Box 40811–00100, Nairobi, to be liquidator of Drumvale Farmers Co-operative Society Limited (CS/998) (in liquidation), for a period not exceeding one year, and whereas the said Peter Wanjohi Kiama, Chief Co-operative Officer, P.O. Box 40811–00100, Nairobi, has not been able to complete the liquidation.
Now therefore, I extend the period of liquidation with effect from 30th March, 2016, for another period not exceeding one year for the said Peter Wanjohi Kiama, to act as liquidator in the matter of the said co-operative society.
Dated the 30th March, 2016.
P. N. GICHUKI,
MR/8858461     Ag. Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
