THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
(Cap. 446)
THE NATIONAL WATER CONSERVATION AND PIPELINE CORPORATION ORDER
(L.N. 270 of 1988)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by paragraph 3 of the National Water Conservation and Pipeline Corporation Order, 1988, the Cabinet Secretary for Environment, Water and Natural Resources appoints—
Principal Secretary, the National Treasury,
Principal Secretary, State Department of Water and Regional Authorities,
Director Water Services, State Department of Water and Regional Authorities,
to be Members of  the National Water Conservation and Pipeline Corporation, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 17th April, 2015.
Dated the 16th May, 2015.
JUDI W. WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary,
Environment, Water and Natural Resources.
