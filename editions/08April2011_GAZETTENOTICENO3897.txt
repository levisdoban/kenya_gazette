NOTICE is given that the preparation of the above-mentioned part development plan is ready for inspection.

Copies of the part development plan as prepared have been deposited for public inspection at the offices of the Director of City Planning, City Hall, 2nd Floor, Forward Planning Section notice board.
The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the offices of Director of City Planning, City Hall, 2nd Floor, Forward Planning Section notice board, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.

Box 30075, Nairobi, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
