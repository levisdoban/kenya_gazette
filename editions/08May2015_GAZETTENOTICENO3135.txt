THE JUDICATURE ACT
(Cap. 8)
THE HIGH COURT (PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE) RULES
Corrigenda
Gazette Notice No. 53 of 2013 be and is amended by:
Deleting Rules 1 (1), (2), (3) and (4) and inserting the following in substitution thereof:
(1) The sittings of the High Court shall be three in every year.
(2) Such sittings other than in the Coast Province shall be as follows—
(a) From the 14th January to the second Wednesday before Good Friday.
(b) From the Wednesday after Easter week to 31st July; and
(c)  From 16th September to 20th December.
(3) Sittings in the Coast Province shall be as follows –
(a) From 5th February to the Friday before Good Friday.
(b) From the Wednesday after Easter week to 31st July; and
(c)  From 19th August to 20th December.
(4) Despite sub-rule (1), (2) and (3), the Chief Justice may determine sittings of the Court which sittings shall be advertised and notified in the matter as the Chief Justice may direct.
Dated the 5th May, 2015.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice/President Supreme Court of Kenya.
