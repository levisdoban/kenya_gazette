THE REGISTERED LAND ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Wamugunda Kabiru, is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Daiga/Umande Block 4/754 (Nyariginu), situate in Laikipia District, and whereas sufficient evidence adduced proves that the said land title was fraudulently acquired, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the said land title for cancellation have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received, I intend to dispense with production of the said land title deed and give a new title deed to the rightful owner, Samson Ngure Gikandi, and upon such publication the land title issued earlier shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
