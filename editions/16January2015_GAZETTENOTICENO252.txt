
				Page: 79GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 252
THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002)
NAIROBI CITY WATER AND SEWERAGE COMPANY LIMITED
Submission of Comments on the Water Tariff Adjustment
NOTICE is given to the general public that the Athi Water Services Board (AWSB) which provides water services by authority of a license issued by the Water Services Regulatory Board (WASREB) to the City of Nairobi through Nairobi City Water and Sewerage Company Limited have applied to the Regulator for a water tariff adjustment.
(a) The tariff adjustment is for the period 2014/15 to 2017/18.
(b) AWSB proposes an increasing block tariff (IBT) structure with a built in component of a life line tariff of KSh. 204 per month for  water consumption of up to 6000 litres per month for piped connections and regulated resale by manned water  kiosks vendors at KSh. 1 per 20 litre container.
(c)  The tariff adjustment is to enable the Nairobi City Water and Sewerage Company Limited attain full cost recovery, servicing of infrastructural development loan, undertake minor investments in water and sewerage  services  and generally  improve service delivery.
The public is invited to visit our website to view details of the proposed  water tariff adjustments within the next thirty (30) days   and submit comments. Written comments should be addressed by letter or e-mail to the: Chief Executive Officer, Water Services Regulatory Board, P.O. Box 41621–00100 Nairobi. e-maIl: Info@wasreb.go.ke or tariff@wasreb.go.ke .
The closing date for such comments shall be on 12th February, 2015.
