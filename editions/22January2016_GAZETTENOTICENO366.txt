THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT CO-ORDINATION ACT
(No. 1 of 2013)
ESTABLISHMENT OF SUB-COUNTY SERVICE DELIVERY
UNITS
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 14 (1) and (3) of the National Government Co-ordination Act, 2013, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government, with the approval of the His Excellency Hon. Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, establishes the areas specified in the Schedule as National Government Service Delivery Co-ordination Units in the Wajir West Sub-county for the purposes of the Act.
Schedule
Ward                                  Athibolol
Location                             Jagahir
                                           Baaji
                                           Garwen
                                           Sankay Bagalla
                                           Degayar
                                           Bula Rasmi
Sub-location                       Samatar
Dated the 22nd January, 2016.
JOSEPH NKAISSERY,
Cabinet Secretary,
Ministry of Interior and Co-ordination of National Government.
