THE UNIVERSITIES ACT
(No. 42 of 2012)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 38 (1) (a), of the Universities Act, 2012, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Vimal Shah
to be the Chancellor of Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology for a period of five (5) years.
Dated the 19th May, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
