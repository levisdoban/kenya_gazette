THE INDUSTRIAL COURT ACT
(No. 20 of 2011)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 24 (b) of the Industrial Court Act, 2011, the Chief Justice appoints the following members of the Employment and Labour Relations Rules Committee for a period of five (5) years with effect from 1st September, 2015.
Beatrice Opolo—Federation of Kenya Employers (FKE).
Daisy Wekesa Nabututu—Central Organisation of Trade Unions (COTU) (K).
The APPOINTMENT of Gladwell Mumia and Jeniffer Wanjiku vide GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 5711 of  2014 is revoked and replaced accordingly.
Dated the 31st August, 2015.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice and President, Supreme Court of Kenya.
