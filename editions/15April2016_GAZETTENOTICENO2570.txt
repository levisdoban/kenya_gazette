REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Muthike Githacuri (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.22 acre or thereabouts, known as Muguga/Gitaru/T. 242, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the principal magistrate’s court at Kikuyu in succession cause No. 175 of 2015, has issued grant of letters of administration to Wangari Kinyanya Kungu, and whereas the land title deed issued earlier to the said Muthike Githacuri (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Muthike Githacuri (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
