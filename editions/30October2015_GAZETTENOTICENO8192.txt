THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Victor Ongaro Nyawiri, of Rawinji village within Oyugis Town in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of land containing 0.25 hectare or thereabouts, known as Central Kasipul/Kamuma/443, and whereas the High Court of Kenya at Kisii in succession cause No. H.C.C. 29 of 2009 (O.S), has issued a decree de-registering the proprietorship of the said parcel of land and replacing the ownership with James Ajwang Odiyo, and whereas efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the land title deed have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said change of ownership to James Ajwang Odiyo, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 30th October, 2015.
J. OSIOLO,
MR/8477294         Land Registrar, Rachuonyo North/South Districts.
