THE PHYSICAL PLANNING ACT
(Cap. 286)
Completion of Part Development Plan
(Land Use Plan for Tana Delta)
NOTICE is given that the above-mentioned part development plan was on 15th December, 2014, completed.
The part development plan relates to land situated within the Tana Delta Sub-County, Tana River County.
Copies of the part development plan has been deposited for public inspection at the County Physical Planning Office and Tana Delta Sub-County Administrator’s office.
The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at offices of County Physical Planning Office and Tana Delta Sub-County Administrator’s office, between the hours of 7.30 a.m. to 16.30 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objection to the above-named part development plans may send such representations or objections in writing to be received by the County Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 201, Hola, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
Dated the 9th February, 2015.
MORRIS OCHIENG,
