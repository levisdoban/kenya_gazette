TASKFORCE ON IMPLEMENTATION OF THE AGRICULTURE, FISHERIES AND FOOD AUTHORITY (AFFA)
REVOCATION OF APPOINTMENT
IT IS notified for the information of the general public that the Acting Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries, has revoked the appointment of the taskforce to operationalize the Agriculture, Fisheries and Food Authority (AFFA) also known as interim Management Committee (IMC)* with effect from 15th June, 2015.
Dated the 14th July, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
Ag. Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture,
Livestock and Fisheries.

GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 5279
TASKFORCE ON IMPLEMENTATION OF THE KENYA AGRICULTURE AND LIVESTOCK RESEARCH ORGANIZATION (KALRO)
REVOCATION OF APPOINTMENT
IT IS notified for the information of the general public that the Acting Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries, has revoked the appointment of the taskforce to operationalize the Kenya Agriculture and Livestock Research Organization (KALRO) also known as Interim Management Committee (IMC)* with effect from 15th June, 2015.
Dated the 14th July, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
Ag. Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture,
Livestock and Fisheries.
*G.N. 1363/2014.
