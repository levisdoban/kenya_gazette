Declaration of Person Elected to the National Assembly—Kabete Constituency By-Election
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 88 (4), 97 (1) (a), 101 (4), 103 (1) (a) of the Constitution, section 4 of the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission Act, 2011, Sections 16, 38 and 39 the Elections Act, 2011 and Regulations 87 4 (b) of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012 the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission hereby declares that the person whose name is listed in the Schedule hereunder was elected as member to the National Assembly representing Kabete Constituency having received the majority of the votes cast in the by-election held on 4th May, 2015and complied with the provisions of the Constitution, Elections Act, 2011 and Elections (General) Regulations(2012).
