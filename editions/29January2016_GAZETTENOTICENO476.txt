PURSUANT to section 28 of the Sacco Societies Act, as read with regulation 8 of the Sacco Societies (Deposit-Taking Sacco Business) Regulations, 2010, the Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority (Authority), publishes for the notification of the general public:
(a) the list of Sacco Societies which have been duly licensed to carry out deposit-taking Sacco business in Kenya in accordance with section 26 (1) of the Act for the financial year ending on 31st December, 2016 as appears in Schedule I, and
(b) the list of Sacco Societies granted restricted deposit-taking licenses in accordance with section 26 (3) of the Act for a period of six (6) months, ending on 30th June, 2016 as appears in Schedule II herein.
