GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 698
THE KENYA INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS ACT
 (No. 2 of 1998)
DECLARATION OF VACANCIES
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6B (1) (a) of the Kenya Information and Communications Act, the Cabinet Secretary for information, Communications and Technology declares vacancies in the positions of members of the Board of the Communications Authority of Kenya and invites applications from suitably qualified persons for the positions of members of the Board of the Communications Authority of Kenya. The applications shall be forwarded to the selection panel pursuant to section 6B (4) of the Act, within seven days of publication of this notice
Dated the 4th February, 2016.
JOE MUCHERU,
Cabinet Secretary for Information,
Communications and Technology.
