THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
NOTIFICATION OF SPECIAL SITTINGS OF THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to Standing Order No. 29 (3) of the  Standing Orders of the NationalAssembly, it is notified for the information of Members of the National Assembly and the general public that four special sittings of the Assembly shall be held in the National Assembly Chamber in the Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi, on Wednesday, 16th December, 2015 and Thursday, 17th December, 2015 at 9.30 a.m and 2.30 p.m on both days, for purposes of:
(a)  Consideration of Special Motions for appointment of persons to the offices of chairperson and members of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission;
(b) Consideration of Special Motions for appointment of persons as Cabinet Secretaries;
(c)  Consideration of Special Motions for appointment of persons as Principal Secretaries;
(d) Consideration of Bills to implement various Articles of the Constitution, with an enactment deadline of 27th August, 2016.
Dated the 4th December, 2015.
JUSTIN MUTURI,
                                                          Speaker, National Assembly.
