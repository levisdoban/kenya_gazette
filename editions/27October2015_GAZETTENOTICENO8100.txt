
				Page: 2571GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 8100
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
THE SENATE STANDING ORDERS
CONVENING OF A SITTING OF THE SENATE
NOTICE is given to all Senators that—
PURSUANT to section 33 (3) (a) of the County Governments Act and standing order 68 (1) (a) of the Senate Standing Orders;
AND FURTHER UPON receipt of a letter from the Speaker of the County Assembly of Murang’a dated 21st October, 2015 and received in the Office of the Speaker of the Senate on 22nd October, 2015, by which the Speaker of the County Assembly of Murang’a informed the Speaker of the Senate of the approval of a Motion for the removal from office by impeachment of Mwangi wa Iria, the Governor of Murang’a County;
I convene a sitting of the Senate on Wednesday, 28th October, 2015.
The sitting shall be held in the Senate Chamber, Main Parliament Buildings, Ground Floor, Nairobi, commencing at 11:00 a.m. The business to be transacted at the sitting shall be the hearing of the charges against Mwangi wa Iria, the Governor of Murang’a County.
Dated the 27th October, 2015.
EKWEE ETHURO,
Speaker of the Senate.
