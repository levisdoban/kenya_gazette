GAZETTE NOTICE NO.  9118
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE VIHIGA COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
EXTENSION OF THE ASSEMBLY SITTINGS
PURSUANT to Article 196 of the Constitution of Kenya, 2010 and Standing Order No. 25 (2) and (4) of the Vihiga County Assembly Standing Orders, it is notified for the information of the Members of the County Assembly and the general public that the County Assembly shall extend its sittings from 4th of December, 2015 to 18th of December, 2015 to conduct crucial business before the Assembly.
Dated the 2nd December, 2015.
D. C. CHITWAH,
                                             Speaker, County Assembly of Vihiga.
