REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Robert Githaiga Mwangi (deceased), is the registered proprietor of that piece of land containing 4.68 hectares or thereabout, known as Daiga/Ethi Block 2/157, situate in Laikipia District, and whereas the Chief Magistrate’s Court of Kenya at Kiambu in succession cause No. 109 of 2012 has issued grant letters of administration to Margaret Wanjiru Githaiga, and whereas the said land title deed issued earlier to the said Robert Githaiga Mwangi (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received, I intend to dispense with production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Rober Githaiga Mwangi (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
