THE EAST AFRICAN COMMUNITY CUSTOMS MANAGEMENT ACT, 2004
Appointment and Limits of Transit Shed, Customs Areas etc.
PURSUANT to section 12 (1) of the East African Community Customs Management Act, 2004, the Commissioner of Customs Services appoints:
The place specified in the first column of the First Schedule, as a transit shed for the purposes of the Act and limits shall be those set out in the second column of the Schedule;
The places specified in the first column of the Second Schedule of Customs areas for the purposes set out in the third column of the Schedule, limits shall be those set out in the second column of that Schedule.
