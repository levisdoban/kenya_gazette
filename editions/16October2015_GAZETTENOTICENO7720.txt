THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT

THE BETTING, LOTTERIES AND GAMING ACT
(Cap. 131)
APPOINTMENT.
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (1) (e) of the Betting, Lotteries and Gaming Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government appoints—
Victor Prengei Sitienei,
Paul Njaga,
Peter Kanaiya,
Simon Chelugui,
Maryanne Kirubi Musangi,
to be members of the Betting Control and Licensing Board, for a period of three (3) years with effect from 2nd October, 2015 and revokes the appointment of Kiprono Kittony, Kennedy Busisa, Peter Mbiu Muting’a, Paschalia S. Mbato and Janet N. Ogeto.
Dated the 2nd October, 2015.
JOSEPH NKAISSERY,
Cabinet Secretary,
Ministry of Interior and Co-ordination of National Government.
