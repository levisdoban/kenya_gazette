THE KENYA LITERATURE BUREAU ACT
(Cap. 209)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (1) (a) of the Kenya Literature Bureau Act I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Francis Baya
to be the Chairperson, Kenya Literature Bureau, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 18th  March, 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
