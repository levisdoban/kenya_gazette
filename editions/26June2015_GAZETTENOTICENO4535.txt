THE LAW OF SUCCESSION ACT
(Cap. 160)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 47 of the Succession Act, the Chief Justice appoints—
DIANA K. MOCHACHE
Principal Magistrate, to represent the High Court in succession matters in the areas of jurisdiction of her current court station and future magistrate stations deployed to from time to time, with effect from 1st June, 2015.
 
Dated the 29th May, 2015.
WILLY MUTUNGA, Chief Justice/President. Supreme Court of Kenya.
