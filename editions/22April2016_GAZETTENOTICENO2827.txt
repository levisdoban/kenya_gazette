THE ADVOCATES ACT
THE COMPLAINTS COMMISSION
 100TH QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT to section 53 (9) of the Advocates Act and Rule 12(1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st October 2015 to 31st December, 2015, the Commission received a total of two hundred and twenty six (226) new complaints out of which twenty three (23) were classified and files opened. Two hundred and three (203) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
