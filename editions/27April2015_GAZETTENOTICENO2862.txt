THE HOUSING ACT
 (Cap. 117)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (2) of the Housing Act, the Cabinet Secretary, the Ministry of Lands, Housing and Urban Development appoints—

Lands, Housing and Urban Development.
