THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Nakuru Kiamunyeki Company Limited, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 3.370 hectares or thereabout, known as Dundori/Lanet Block 5/270 (Kiamunyeki ‘A’), situate in the district of Nakuru, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court in civil case No. 508 of 2011, has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Nancy Wamboi, and whereas the registered proprietor has refused to surrender the said land title deed in respect of the piece of land, notice is hereby given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the land title deed and issue a new title deed to the said Nancy Wamboi, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Nakuru Kiamunyeki Company Limited, shall be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 30th October, 2015.
C. W. SUNGUTI,
MR/8477187                               Land Registrar, Nakuru District.
