THE COMPETITION ACT
(No. 12 of 2010)
NOTICE OF SETTLEMENT
IT IS notified for general information in accordance with section 39 of the Competition Act, 2010 (“the Act”), that in exercise of the powers conferred by section 38 of the Competition Act, the Competition Authority of Kenya (“the authority”) has entered into a settlement agreement with Consumer Link Communication Limited (“Company”)
Under the settlement agreement—
(a) the Authority imposed a fine of one million two hundred thousand shillings (KSh. 1,200,000) only on the Company; and
(b) the Company gave a written undertaking to all times in future conduct its business in compliance with the Act.
Dated the 15th October, 2015.
WANG’OMBE KARIUKI,
Director-General.
