THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE SIAYA COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
Special Sitting of the County Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Order No. 27 (3) of the County Assembly of Siaya Standing Orders, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly and the general public that there will be a Special Sitting of the County Assembly which shall be held in the County Assembly Chambers, in Siaya on Wednesday, 9th September, 2015 at 9.30 a.m. for purposes of swearing in of Ouma Omar Shaban as the Member of the County Assembly of Siaya representing North Ugenya Ward as per Gazzette Notice No. 6229 dated 24th August, 2015.
Dated the 3rd September, 2015.
GEORGE O. OKODE,
MR/8045661                  Speaker of the County Assembly of Siaya.
