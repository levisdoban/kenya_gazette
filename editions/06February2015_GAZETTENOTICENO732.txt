THE INTERIM KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to Standing Order 26 (3) of the Interim Standing Orders of the Kiambu County Assembly, it is notified for the information of Members of the Kiambu County Assembly that a special sitting of the Assembly shall be held in the Assembly Chamber in the Main Assembly Buildings, Kiambu, on Friday, 6th February, 2015 at 9.30 a.m. for purposes of tabling the Kiambu County Supplementary Budget for the year ending 30th June, 2015.
Dated the 4th February, 2015.
GATHII IRUNGU,
MR/6901132              Speaker of the County Assembly of Kiambu.
