THE ANTI-CORRUPTION AND ECONOMIC CRIMES ACT
(No. 3 of 2003)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (1) of the Anti-Corruption and Economic Crimes Act, the Chief Justice appoints—
JESSE NYAGAH
 
Chief Magistrate, as a special magistrare to preside over cases involving corruption and economic crimes in the areas of jurisdiction of his current court station and future stations deployed to from time to time, with effect from 1st June, 2015.
 
Dated the 29th May, 2015.
WILLY MUTUNGA, Chief Justice/President, Supreme Court of Kenya.
