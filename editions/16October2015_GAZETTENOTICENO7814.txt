THE ADVOCATES ACT
THE COMPLAINTS COMMISSION
99TH QUARTERLY REPORT
PURSUANT to section 53(9) of the Advocates Act and Rule 12(1) of the Advocates (Complaints Commission) Rules, 1991 it is notified for general information that from 1st July, 2015 to 30th September, 2015, the Commission received a total of two hundred and twenty five (225) new complaints out of which thirty nine (39) were classified and files opened. One hundred and eighty six (186) were submitted for further enquiry under Preliminary Enquiry Initiative.
