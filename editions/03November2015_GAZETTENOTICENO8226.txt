THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF BOMET
SPECIAL SITTING
PURSUANT to Standing Order 29 of the Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Bomet, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly of Bomet and the general public that the Assembly shall have a special sitting on Thursday,  5th November, 2015, at 10.00 a.m. at the County Assembly Chamber in Bomet for the tabling and consideration of the request by the County Executive Member for Finance for an Overdraft Facility.
Dated the 3rd November, 2015.
G. K. KIPNG’ETICH,
Speaker.
