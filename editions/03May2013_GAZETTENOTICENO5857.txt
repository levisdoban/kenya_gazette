THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 33)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Christopher Ondayo Andhendha, of Ukwala in the Republic of Kenya, is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as East Ugenya/Murumba/109, situate in the district of Siaya, and whereas summons under section B of RLA has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Zacharia Oduor Okoth, and whereas the executive officer of the said court has executed an instrument of transfer in favour of Zacharia Oduor Okoth, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the land title deed issued to the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of transfer and issue a land title deed to the said Zacharia Oduor Okoth, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Christopher Ondayo Andhendha, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 3rd May, 2013.
P. A. NYANJA,
MR/1933095                 Land Registrar, Ugenya/Ugunja Districts.
