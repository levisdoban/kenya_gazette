THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(CAP. 300, SECTION 33)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Jacob Karwamba M’Limberia, is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Njia/Ciamwenda/3224, situate in the district of Meru North in the Republic of Kenya, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court in civil suit No. 44 of 2008 has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Mbaabu M’Limungi, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land has failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of sub-division and transfer and issue a land title deed to the said Mbaabu M’Limungi, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Jacob Karwamba M’Limberia, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 7th June, 2013.
N. MUNGAI,
MR/2519380                         Land Registrar, Meru North Dictrict.
