THE STANDARDS ACT
(Cap. 496)
THE STANDARDS TRIBUNAL
Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 16A (2) of the Standards Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Industrialization and Enterprise Development appoints—
Gladys Muthoni Mburu (Ms.)
to be Chairman of the Standards Tribunal, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 11th August, 2015.
Dated the 11th August, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
                  Cabinet Secretary for Industrialization and Enterprise Development.
