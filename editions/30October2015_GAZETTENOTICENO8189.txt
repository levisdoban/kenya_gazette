THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Salina Jesang Kasi, of P.O. Box 48, Serem in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 2.04 hectares or thereabout, known as Nandi/Koibarak ‘B’/252, situate in the district of Nandi, and whereas the principal magistrate’s court at Kapsabet in LDT No. 13 of 2011, has issued  a decree that the land title deed issued on 12th May, 2006, be revoked and the title deed reverted to its original owner, Benjamin Kiptanui Kitur, and whereas efforts made to compel Salina Jesang Kasi to surrender the earlier issued land title deed have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of sixty (60) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed, and issue another land title deed to Benjamin Kiptanui Kitur, upon such registration the title deed issued earlier to the said Salina Jesang Kasi, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 30th October, 2015.
