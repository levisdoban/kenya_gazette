THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE TEACHERS SERVICE COMMISSION ACT
(NO. 20 OF 2012)
CONSTITUTION OF SELECTION PANEL
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred on me by section 8 (1) of the Teachers Service Commission Act, 2012, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces declare vacancies in five (5) offices of members of the Teachers Service Commission and proceed to constitute a Selection Panel for purposes of selecting suitable candidates as follows—
Karega Mutahi (Prof.)
George I. Godia (Prof.)
John Kipkorir
Esther Muhanji
Joseph Chirchir
Jean Njeri Muhoho
This Panel is constituted for purpose of selecting suitable candidates for appointment as members of the Commission, as per the provisions of section 8 (2).
Further, by this notice I revoke Gazette Notice No. 9396 of 2013.
Dated the 16th July, 2014.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
