
				Page: 1901GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 3017
THE REFUGEES ACT
(No. 13 of 2006)
REVOCATION OF PRIMA FACIE REFUGEE STATUS
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (3) of the Refugees Act, 2006, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government revokes the prima facie refugee status of asylum seekers from Somalia with effect from 1st April, 2016, and all asylum seekers from Somalia shall be required to undergo the Refugee Status Determination process as prescribed in the Regulations.
Dated the 27th April, 2016.
JOSEPH NKAISSERRY,
Cabinet Secretary for Interior
and Co-ordination of National Government.
