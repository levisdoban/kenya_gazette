THE EXPORT PROCESSING ZONES ACT
(Cap. 517)
THE EXPORT PROCESSING ZONES AUTHORITY
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (1) of the Export Processing Zones Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Industrialization and Enterprise Development appoints—
Fanuel Odede Kidenda
to be the Chief Executive Officer of the Export Processing Zones Authority, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 21st September, 2015.
Dated the 21st September, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
Cabinet Secretary for Industrialization and Enterprise Development.
