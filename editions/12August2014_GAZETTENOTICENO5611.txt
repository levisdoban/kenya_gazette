GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 5611
THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY
THE INTERIM KIAMBU COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY
PURSUANT to the Standing Order 26 (3) of the Standing Orders of the Kiambu County Assembly, it is notified for the information of the Members of the Kiambu County that a special sitting of the Assembly, shall be held in the Kiambu County Chamber in the Main Assembly Buildings, Kiambu, on Wednesday, 13th August, 2014, at 9.30 a.m., for purposes of consideration of a Motion relating to the Vote on Account.
Dated the 11th August, 2014
GATHII IRUNGU,
Speaker of the County Assembly.
