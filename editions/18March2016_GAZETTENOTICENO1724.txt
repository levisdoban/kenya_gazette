THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
(Cap. 446)
THE COPYRIGHT ACT 
(Cap. 130)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Section 7(3) of the State Corporations Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Millicent Ogutu
to be a member and Chairperson of the Kenya Copyright Board, up to 2nd September, 2015, effective from the 18th March, 2016 and revoke the appointment of Tom Mshindi as Chairperson*.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
*G.N. 7848/2015.
