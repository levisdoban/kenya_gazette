Declaration of Person Elected to Trans Nzoia County Assembly: Matumbei Ward
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Articles 88(4), 193 and 194 of the Constitution, section 4 of the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission Act, 2011, sections 19, 20, 22, 25, 26, 38 and 39 of the Elections Act, 2011 and Regulations 83, 87 (2) and 87 4(b) of the Elections (General) Regulations, 2012, the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission declares that the person whose name is listed in the Schedule hereunder was elected as Member for Trans Nzoia County Assembly – Matumbei Ward having received the majority of the votes cast in the by- election held on 29thDecember, 2014 and complied with the provisions of the Constitution, Elections Act, 2011 and Elections (General) Regulations (2012).
