THE COMPETITION ACT
 (NO. 12 OF 2010)
PROPOSED ACQUISITION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 46 (6) (a) (ii) of the Competition Act, the Competition Authority authorizes the proposed acquisition of the entire issued share capital of Nutro Manufacturing (EPZ) Limited and assets used for the processing of nutritional products by Orbit Chemical Industries Limited and Ariel (EPZ) Limited.
 Dated the 21st May, 2014.
WANG’OMBE KARIUKI,
Director-General.
