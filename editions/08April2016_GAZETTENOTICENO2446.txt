THE PHYSICAL PLANNING ACT
(Cap. 286)
COMPLETION OF DEVELOPMENT PLANS
Part Development Plan No: KAJ/166/2015/02 for (A) Proposed site for construction of High Court Judge and Resident Magistrate’s residence; (B) Proposed site for construction of Kajiado High Court.
NOTICE  is given that above mention part development plans were completed on 30th October, 2015.
The part development plan relates to land situated within Kajiado Township.
Copies of the part development plan have been deposited for public inspection at the offices of the Governor-Kajiado, County Commissioner, Kajiado, County Executive Member for Lands and Physical planning, and office of the County Physical Planning Officer, Kajiado.
The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all interested persons at the offices of the Governor-Kajiado, County Commissioner-Kajiado, County Executive Member for Lands and Physical planning and County Physical Planning Office-Kajiado between 8.00am to 5.00pm, Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection or objection to the above-named Part Development Plan may send such representation in connection or objections in writing to be received by the County Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 11–01100, Kajiado, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
Dated the 9th November, 2015.
ISAAC PARASHINA,
for Director of Physical Planning.
