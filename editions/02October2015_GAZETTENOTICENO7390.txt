 THE KENYA NUCLEAR ELECTRICITY BOARD ORDER, 2012

Stephen Mwangi Karanja,
Anderson Kipkoech (Dr),
Josephine Odira Sinyo,

Dated the 2nd October 2015.
HENRY ROTICH,
Ag. Cabinet Secretary, Energy and Petroleum.
