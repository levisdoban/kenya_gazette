THE CONSTITUENCIES DEVELOPMENT FUND ACT, 2013
Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (2) (d) of the Constituencies Development Fund Act, 2013, the Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Devolution and Planning appoints—
Elias Mbau
as a member of the Board of the Constituencies Development Fund, with effect from 1st January, 2015, for a period three (3) years.
Dated the 7th January, 2015.
ANNE WAIGURU,
Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Devolution and Planning.
