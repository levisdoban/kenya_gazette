THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE NATIONAL COHESION AND INTEGRATION ACT
 (No. 12 of 2008)
APPOINTMENT OF CHAIRPERSON
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 17 (1) (a) and (b) of the National Cohesion and Integration Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces appoint—
Francis Xavier ole Kaparo
as the Chairperson of the National Cohesion and Integration Commission, for a period of three (3) years.
Dated the 12th August, 2014.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
