THE CIVIL PROCEDURE ACT
(Cap. 21)
THE MEDIATION (PILOT PROJECT) RULES, 2015
PURSUANT to section 59 A of the Civil Procedure Act and Rule 1 of The Mediation (Pilot Project) Rules, 2015, it is declared that The Mediation (Pilot Project) Rules shall take effect from 4th April, 2016 and shall thereafter subsist for an initial period of six (6) months subject to extension.
Dated the 16th March, 2016.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice/President Supreme Court of Kenya.
