THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(NO. 3 OF 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Titus Gitobu Peter (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nkuene/L-Mikumbune/1901, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court in succession cause No. 66 of 2014, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of Susan Maringa Peter, and whereas the said court has executed and application to be registered as proprietor by transmission RL. 19 and whereas the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land registered in the name of Titus Gitobu Peter (deceased), is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission RL. 19 in the name of Susan Maringa Peter, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Titus Gitobu Peter (deceased) shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 6th June, 2014.
B. K. KAMWARO,
