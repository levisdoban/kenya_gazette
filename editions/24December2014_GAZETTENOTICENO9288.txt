THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
THE SENATE STANDING ORDERS
Special Sitting of the Senate
PURSUANT to standing order 29 of the Senate Standing Orders, on the request of the Senate Minority Leader supported by fifteen (15) Senators, I have appointed Tuesday, 30th December, 2014 as a day for a special sitting of the Senate. The special sitting shall be held in the Senate Chamber, Ground Floor, Main Parliament Buildings, Nairobi, at 2.30 p.m.
The business to be transacted at the special sitting shall be a Motion relating to the matter of the introduction, debate, passage and the enactment of the Security Laws (Amendment) Bill, (National Assembly Bills No. 39 of 2014) which was passed by the National Assembly on Thursday, 18th December, 2014.
In accordance with standing order 29(5) of the Senate Standing Orders, the business specified in this notice shall be the only business before the Senate during the special sitting, following which the Senate shall stand adjourned until Tuesday, 10th February, 2015, at 2:30 p.m. in accordance with the Resolution of the Senate made on Thursday, 4th December, 2014.
Dated the 23rd December, 2014.
