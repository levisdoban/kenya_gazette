THE STATE CORPORATIONS ACT
(Cap. 446)
Re-Appointment
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 6 (2) of the State Corporations Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, re-appoint—
Martin Kariuki Muragu
to be the Chairperson of Industrial and Commercial Development Corporation (ICDC) for a further period of one (1) year, with effect from the 10th September, 2015.
Dated the 28th August, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
