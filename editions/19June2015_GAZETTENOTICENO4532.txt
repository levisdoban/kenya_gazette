THE CENTRAL BANK OF KENYA ACT
(Cap. 491)
APPOINTMENT OF DEPUTY GOVERNOR
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 13B of the Central Bank of Kenya Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, appoint—
Sheila M’Mbijjewe
to be the Deputy Governor of the Central Bank of Kenya, for a period of four (4) years, with effect from the 19th June, 2015.
Dated the 19th June, 2015.
 
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
