THE REGISTERED LAND ACT
(NO. 3 OF 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Naomi Waithera Mwaniki, of Nyandarua in the Republic of Kenya, is the registered proprietor of that piece of land known as Nyandarua/Mawingo Salient/139, situate in Nyandarua District, and whereas the High Court at Nakuru in succession cause No. 388 of 2011, has ordered that the grant of letters of administration issued to Naomi Waithera Mwaniki, the administrator of the estate of Simon Mwaniki Ngugi (deceased) be annulled and the same revert to Simon Mwaniki Ngugi (deceased), as the registered proprietor, and whereas all efforts made to compel the registered proprietor to surrender the said land title deed to the registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received, I intend to dispense with production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of instrument of court order, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Naomi Waithera Mwaniki, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 11th July, 2014.
N. G. GATHAIYA,
MR/5019702         Land Registrar, Nyandarua/Samburu Districts.
