THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
Registration of Instrument
WHEREAS Mary Adhiambo Otieno, of Siaya in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 1.3 hectares or thereabout, known as Siaya/Mur-Ngiya/548, situate in the district of Siaya, and whereas the land registrar has established that the land title deed was acquired fraudulently, and whereas all efforts made to recover the land title deed issued by the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and revert it to (1) Samuel Odhiambo Ondongo and (2) Walter Otieno Odongo, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Mary Adhiambo Otieno, shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 11th December, 2014.
P. A. OWEYA,
MR/6254718                                  Land Registrar, Siaya District.
Gazette Notice No. 8967
THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
Registration of Instrument
WHEREAS Paul Onyango Olombe (deceased), of Siaya in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 1.52 hectares or thereabout, known as Central Alego/Nyalgunga/2668, situate in the district of Siaya, and whereas the High Court at Kisumu in succession cause No. H.C./R.M/372 of 2014 has ordered that the said piece of land be registered in the names of (1) Meshack Ouma Dero and (2) Naman Oluoch Dero, and whereas all efforts made to recover the land title deed issued thereof by the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said grant document and issue a land title deed to the said (1) Meshack Ouma Dero and (2) Naman Oluoch Dero, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Paul Onyango Olombe (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 11th December, 2014.
P. A. OWEYA,
MR/6254891                                  Land Registrar, Siaya District.
