THE ADVOCATES ACT
(Cap. 16)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 53 (1) of the Advocates Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Naomi Wagereka (Ms.)
to be a Commissioner of the Advocates Complaints Commission, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 1st February, 2016.
Dated the 4th January, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
