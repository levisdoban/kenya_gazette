THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(CAP. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
(EXTENSION ORDER)
WHEREAS by liquidation order dated 19th February, 2015, I appointed Raphael K. Maseki, Senior Co-operative Auditor, Headquarters, to be liquidator of Posta Investment Co-operative Society Limited (CS/8494) (in liquidation), for a period not exceeding one year, and whereas the said Raphael K. Maseki, Senior Co-operative Auditor, Headquarters, has not been able to complete the liquidation.
Now therefore, I extend the period of the liquidation with effect from 19th February, 2016, for another period not exceeding one (1) year for the said Raphael K. Maseki, Senior Co-operative Auditor, Headquarters to act as liquidator in the matter of the said co-operative society.
Dated the 19th February, 2016.
P. N. GICHUKI,
MR/8858219     Ag. Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
