REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Francis Njiraini (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.365 hectare or thereabouts, known as Dagoretti/Thogoto/2474, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the chief magistrate’s court at Kiambu in succession cause No. 288 of 2011 has issued grant of letters of administration to Serah Njoki Njiraini, and whereas the land title deed issued earlier to Francis Njiraini (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Francis Njiraini (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
