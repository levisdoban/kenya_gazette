THE PRISONS ACT
(Cap. 90)
RE-NAMING OF PRISON STATION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 24 (3) of the Prisons Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of the National Government re-names Athi River GK Prison as GK Prison Kitengela.
Dated the 24th March, 2016.
JOSEPH NKAISSERY,
Cabinet Secretary for Interior
and Co-ordination of the National Government.
