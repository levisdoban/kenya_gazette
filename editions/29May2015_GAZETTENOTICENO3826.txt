THE COMPETITION ACT
(No. 12 of 2010)
EXEMPTION
PURSUANT to section 30 of the Comptetion Act, it is notified for general information that the Competition Authority has exempted the following proposed agreements for a period of 5 years—
(a)   the production and bottling agreement dated 21st August, 2014 between Distell Limited and Kenya Wine Agencies Limited; and
(b)   the supply and distribution agreement dated 21st August, 2014 between Distell Limited and Kenya Wine Agencies Limited.
Dated the 15th May, 2015.
WANG’OMBE KARIUKI,
Director-General.
