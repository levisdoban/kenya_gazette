
				Page: 1181GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 3435
THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 46 (3) (b) of the Water Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Environment, Water and Natural Resources appoints—
Principal Secretary, the National Treasury,
Principal Secretary, State Department of Water and Regional Authorities,
to be Members  of  the Water Services Regulatory Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 17th April, 2015.
Dated the 16th May, 2015.
JUDI W. WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary,
Environment, Water and Natural Resources.
