THE PHYSICAL PLANNING ACT

COMPLETION OF PART DEVELOPMENT PLAN
(PDP No. 326/2011/3 for Existing Residential Plot, Garissa)
NOTICE is given that the preparation of the above-mentioned part development plan was on 1st April, 2011, completed.
The part development plan relates to land situated within Municipal Council of Garissa.
Copies of the part development plan as prepared have been deposited for public inspection at the offices of the District Physical Planning Officer, Garissa, District Commissioner, Garissa, Town Clerk, Municipal Council of Garissa and Chief, Township Location.
The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at the offices of District Physical Planning Officer, Garissa, District Commissioner, Garissa, Town Clerk, Municipal Council of Garissa and Chief, Township Location, between the hours of 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objection to the above-named part development plan may send such representations or objections in writing to be received by the District Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 252–70100, Garissa, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
Dated the 1st April, 2011.
