THE KENYA NATIONAL GUINEA WORM ERADICATION CERTIFICATION COMMITTEE
APPOINTMENT
IT IS notified for the general information of the public that following the Sixty-fourth (64th) World Health Assembly (WHA) Resolutions, WHA 39.21 and WHA 42.29 on elimination of Dracunculiasis and WHA 44.5, WHA 50.35 and WHA 57.9 of eradication of Dracunculiasis, the Cabinet Secretary for Health, has established Guinea Worm Eradication Certification Committee and appoints—
Njeri C. Wamae (Prof.)—(Chairman),
Benson Estambale (Prof.),
David Sang (Dr.),
Benson Muasya,
Francis Dikir,
Pauline Okumu,
Joseph Sitienci (Dr.),
Tatu Kamau (Dr.)—(Secretory)
to be members of the Committee for a period of three (3) years.
Dated the 7th May, 2014.
JAMES MACHARIA, Cabinet Secretary for Health.
