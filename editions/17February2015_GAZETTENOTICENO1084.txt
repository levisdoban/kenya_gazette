THE CONSTITUENCIES DEVELOPMENT FUND ACT

APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (4) of the Constituencies Development Fund Act, 2013, the Cabinet Secretary for Devolution and Planning appoints—

ANNE WAIGURU,
Cabinet Secretary for Devolution and Planning.
