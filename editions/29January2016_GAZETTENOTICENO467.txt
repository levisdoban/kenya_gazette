THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Charles Oruko Obadha (deceased), of Yala in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as North Gem/Ndere/856, situate in the district of Siaya, and whereas the principal magistrate’s court at Siaya in succession cause No. H.C./47 of 2012, has ordered that the piece of land be registered in the name of Alfred Ombus Oruko, and whereas efforts made to recover the land title deed issued thereof by the land registrar have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said grant document and issue a land title deed to the said Alfred Ombus Oruko, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Charles Oruko Obadha (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 5th June, 2014.
P. A. OWEYA,
