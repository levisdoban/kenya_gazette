
				Page: 1182GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 3438
THE WATER ACT
(No. 8 of 2002)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 51 of the Water Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Environment, Water and Natural Resources appoints—
Principal Secretary, State Department of Water and Regional Authorities,
Principal Secretary, the National Treasury,
to be Members  of  the Lake Victoria North Water Services Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 17th April, 2015.
Dated the 16th May, 2015.
JUDI W. WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary,
Environment, Water and Natural Resources.
