THE HOUSING ACT
(Cap. 117)
THE NATIONAL HOUSING CORPORATION
Appointment
IN EXERCISE of powers conferred by section 3 (2) of the Housing Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Land, Housing and Urban Development appoints—
Purity Sein Karariet
to be Director of the National Housing Corporation, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 15th December, 2014.
Dated the 15th December, 2014.
CHARITY KALUKI NGILU,
Cabinet Secretary, for Land, Housing and Urban Development.
