THE AGRICULTURE, FISHERIES AND FOOD AUTHORITY ACT
(No. 13 of 2013)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (1) of the Agriculture, Fisheries and Food Authority Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries appoints—
Under subsections (b–h)—
Principal Secretary, State Department of Agriculture;
Principal Secretary, National Treasury;
Principal Secretary, State Department of Devolution;
Principal Secretary, State Department responsible for Lands;
Principal Secretary, State Department for Environment;
Principal Secretary, Ministry of Industrialization and Enterprise Development;
Abdulkadir A. Khalif, National Land Commission;
Under subsection (k)—
Chairman, Transition Authority;
as members of the Board of Directors of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food Authority, with effect from the 2nd June, 2015.
Dated the 28th May, 2015.
ADAN MOHAMED,
Ag. Cabinet Secretary for Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries.
