NEW WATER AND SEWERAGE TARIFF FOR SIBO WATER AND SANITATION COMPANY LIMITED
THE Water Services Regulatory Board (WASREB) approved newly proposed water and sewerage tariff for implementation by SIBO Water and Sewerage Company Limited in line with section 73 (5) of the Water Act. 2002.
Therefore, Lake Victoria   South Water Services Board (LVWSB) is giving a two months notice to all existing and potential water and sewerage customers of Sibo Water and Sanitation Company ltd that the tariffs have been adjusted as hereunder.
