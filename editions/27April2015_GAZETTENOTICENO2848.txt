
				Page: 1013GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 2848
THE MICRO AND SMALL ENTERPRISES ACT
(No. 55 of 2012)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 30 (1) (a) of the Micro and Small Enterprises Act, 2012, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces of the Republic of Kenya, appoint—
Charles Waithaka
to be the Chairperson of the Micro and Small Enterprises Authority, for a period of three (3) years with effect from the 17th April, 2015. The appointment of Paul Billy Muhia Ngugi, is revoked*
Dated the 17th April, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
*G.N. 2902/2013.
