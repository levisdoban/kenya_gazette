THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE MANDERA COUNTY ASSEMBLY STANDING ORDERS
Special Sitting of the Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Order 26 (3) of the County Assembly of Mandera, it is notified for the information of members of the County Assembly of Mandera that a special sitting of the Assembly shall be held in the County Assembly Chamber in the County Assembly Building, Mandera on Thursday, 29th January, 2015 at 9.00 a.m. for purposes of consideration of the Mandera County FY 2014/2015 Supplementary Budget.
Dated the 25th January, 2015.
ABDIKADIR SHEIKH HASSAN,
                Speaker, Mandera County Assembly.
