THE WATER ACT
(NO. 8 OF 2002)
APPROVED TARIFF STRUCTURE FOR THE PERIOD
 1ST JANUARY, 2011 TO 30TH JUNE, 2013
WATER Services Regulatory Board has approved newly proposed Water and Sewerage tariffs for implementation by Kitui Water and Sanitation Company in line with section 73 (5) of the Water Act 2002.
By extension therefore, Tanathi Water Services Board (TAWSB) gives a two month notice to all existing and potential Water and Sewerage customers of Kitui Water and Sanitation Company and its environs that the tariff has been increased.
As per the Service Provision Agreement (SPA) between TAWSB and Kitui which is approved by the Water Services Regulatory Board the areas of Kitui Municipality, Ikanga, Mutomo, Kamutie, Kanyangi, Kanzui, Ngaaka and Kamunda, Matuu, Yatta, Kithimani, Kyua and the environs or any other beneficiaries of the water supply and Sanitation Services not mentioned, shall pay the approved Water and Sewerage tariff with effect from the date of this notice.
The approved tariff structure shall be as follows:
