COMPLETION OF PART DEVELOPMENT PLAN
(PDP No. 297/2011/02 for Existing Residential Plot)
NOTICE is given that the above-mentioned development plan was completed on 23rd December, 2011.
The development plan relates to land situated within Habaswein Township.
Copies of the part development plan have been deposited for public inspection at the County Physical Planning Office, Habaswein Township.
The copies so deposited are available for inspection free of charge by all persons interested at offices of the District Commissioner Habaswein, offices of chief’s Habaswein Town and District Physical Planning Office, Wajir between the hours of 7.30 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday.
Any interested person who wishes to make any representation in connection with or objection to the above-named part development plan may send such representations or objections in writing to be received by the County Physical Planning Officer, P.O. Box 365, Wajir, within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice and such representation or objection shall state the grounds on which it is made.
