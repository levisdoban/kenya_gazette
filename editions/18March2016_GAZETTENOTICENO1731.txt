
				Page: 999GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 1731
THE EWASO NGIRO SOUTH RIVER BASIN DEVELOPMENT ACT
 (Cap. 447)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 4 (1) (l) of the Ewaso Ngiro South River Basin Development Authority Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Jackson Selela ole Mwanik
to be the Chairman of the Ewaso Ngiro South Development Authority, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 18th March, 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
