1.1                  Sewerage Tariff
(a) Sewerage is charged at 75 per cent of the water billed for all customers with sewer connection.  Disconnected water accounts shall be charged based on the average of the last three months’ sewer charges before the disconnection.
(b) Customers with no water connection:
(i)  Individual Domestic Customers: flat rate of KSh. 300 per month;
(ii) All other customers: 75 per cent of volume of water used as per the metered source of water.
