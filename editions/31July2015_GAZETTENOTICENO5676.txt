GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 5676
THE WILDLIFE CONSERVATION AND MANAGEMENT ACT
(No. 47 of 2013)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 8 (f) (ii) of the Wildlife Conservation and Management Act, 2013, the Cabinet Secretary for Environment and Natural Resources appoints—
Tom Lalampaa;

Dated the 25th June, 2015.
JUDI W. WAKHUNGU,
Cabinet Secretary for Environment and Natural Resources.
