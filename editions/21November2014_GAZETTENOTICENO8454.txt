THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Ikiugu Ajogi (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nyaki/Nkabune/119, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court of Kenya in succession cause No. 305 of 2012 has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of Patrick Koome M’Ikiugi (ID/10593384), and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in respect of the said piece of land registered in the name of Ikiugu Ajogi (deceased), and whereas the land title deed issued in respect of the said piece of land has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in the name of Patrick Koome M’Ikiugi (ID/10593384), and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Ikiugu Ajogi (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
