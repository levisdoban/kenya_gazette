THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(CAP. 490)
APPOINTMENT  OF LIQUIDATOR
(EXTENSION ORDER)
WHEREAS by an extension order dated 13th  May,  2013, I appointed  Lawrence M. Magaju to be liquidator  of New  Mugoiri Fcs. Ltd. (in  liquidation) CS/4647  for  a  period  not  exceeding one year and whereas the  said Lawrence  M.  Magaju, Co-operative Auditor I, Tharaka Nithi County, has not  been able to  complete  the  liquidation.
Now Therefore,  I  extend  the  period  of  the  liquidation  with  effect  from  13th  May, 2014  for  another   period  not  exceeding   one  year   for  the  said Lawrence M. Magaju, Co-operative Auditor 1,Tharaka  Nithi  County, to act as liquidator in the matter of the said Co-operative  Society.
Dated the 26th June, 2014.
P. L. M. MUSYIMI,
MR/5019760    Ag: Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
