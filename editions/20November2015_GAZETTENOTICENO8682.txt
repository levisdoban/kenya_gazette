THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS M’Mutunga Mukaba (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Nkuene/Mitunguu/1084 situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court in succession cause No. 337 of 2011, has issued grant of letters of administration and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of (1) Ashford Mbaabu Mutunga and (2) Alfred Kiambi Mutunga, and whereas the said court has executed an application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19, and whereas the land title deed in respect of M’Mutunga Mukaba is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R. L. 19 in the names of (1) Ashford Mbaabu Mutunga and (2) Alfred Kiambi Mutunga, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said M’Mutunga Mukaba (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 20th November, 2015.
B. K. KAMWARO,
MR/8449362        Land Registrar, Meru District.
