
				Page: 1144GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 2219
THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENTS
WHEREAS Hiram Gacehe Chege (deceased), is registered as proprietor of those pieces of land known as Kabete/Gikuni/T.220 and Kabete/Nyathuna/268, containing 1.1 and 0.25 acre or thereabouts, respectively, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the High Court of Kenya at Nairobi in succession cause No. 3344 of 2003, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) Kahiga Mwaura Gachihi and (2) Serah Njeri Muigai, and whereas the said title deed issued earlier to the said Hiram Gacehe Chege (deceased), has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said instrument of R.L. 19 and R.L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Hiram Gacehe Chege (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 1st April, 2016.
I. N. NJIRU,
MR/8858121                               Land Registrar, Kiambu District.
