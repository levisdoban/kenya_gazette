THE KENYA NATIONAL EXAMINATIONS COUNCIL ACT 
 (No. 29 of 2012)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (1) (a) of the Kenya National Examinations Council Act, 2012, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
George A. O. Magoha (Prof.)
to be the Chairperson of the Kenya National Examinations Council, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 9th March, 2016. The APPOINTMENT of Kabiru Kinyanjui (Prof.) as Chairman of the Council, is revoked with immediate effect.
Dated the 9th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
