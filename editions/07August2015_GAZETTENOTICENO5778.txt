THE INSURANCE ACT
(Cap. 487)
BLUE SHIELD INSURANCE COMPANY LIMITED
(Under Statutory Management)
DECLARATION OF A MORATORIUM
Pursuant to the application by the Commissioner of Insurance in exercise of his powers under section 67 C (3) of the Insurance Act and to the orders of the court subsequently issued on the 21st July, 2015, the period of Statutory Management has been extended for a further period of six (6) months effective 22nd July, 2015.
And further take notice that in exercise of powers conferred by section 67 C (2) (10) of the Insurance Act the Statutory Manager extends the Moratorium on the payments by the said insurer to its policy holders and all other creditors for a period of six (6) months with effect from the date of this notice.
Dated the 22nd July, 2015.
