THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT
(CAP. 490)
APPOINTMENT OF LIQUIDATOR
EXTENSION ORDER
WHEREAS by an order dated 21st October, 2009, I appointed Mr. Daniel Nguti, Senior Co-operative Auditor to be liquidator of Panastaff Sacco Society Limited (In liquidation) CS 2302 for a period not exceeding one year and whereas the said Mr Daniel Nguti, Senior Co-operative Auditor has not been able to complete the liquidation;
 NOW THEREFORE, I  extend the period of the liquidation with effect from 22nd October, 2010 for another period not exceeding one year for the said Mr Daniel Nguti, Senior Co-operative Auditor, Ministry Headquarters to act as liquidator in the matter of the said co-operative society.
Dated the 22nd March, 2010.
F. F. ODHIAMBO,
MR1932053            Commissioner for Co-operative Development.
