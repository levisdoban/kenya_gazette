THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Joseph Mbacia Kagondu (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 0.85 hectare or thereabouts, known as Nginda Samar/Block 2/853, situate in the district of Murang’a, and whereas the High Court at Kitale in succession cause No. 234 of 2005, has issued grant of letters of administration to Irene Wangari Mbacia, and whereas the said title deed issued to Joseph Mbacia Kagondu (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to Joseph Mbacia Kagondu (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
