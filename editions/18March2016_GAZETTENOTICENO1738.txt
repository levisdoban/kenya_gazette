THE TOURISM ACT
(No. 28 of 2011)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 54 (1) (a) of the Tourism Act, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces, appoint—
Betty Addero Radier
to be the Chairperson of the Board of Tourism Research Institute, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 18th March, 2016.
Dated the 17th March, 2016.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
