THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF KWALE
Special Sitting of the Assembly
PURSUANT to Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Kwale, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly of Kwale that a Special Sitting of the Assembly shall be held in the County Assembly Chamber in the County Assembly Buildings, Kwale, on Monday, 19th January, 2015 at 10.00 a.m. for purposes of tabling the Kwale County Supplementary Budget for Financial Year 2014/2015.
Dated the 14th January, 2015.
S. N. RUWA,
MR/6742105           Speaker, County Assembly of Kwale.
