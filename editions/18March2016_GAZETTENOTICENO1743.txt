STEERING COMMITTEE TO ESTABLISH THE NAIROBI METROPOLITAN AREA TRANSPORT AUTHORITY
EXTENSION OF TENURE
IT IS notified for the general information of the public that the Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure has extended the tenure of the Steering Committee to Establish the Nairobi Metropolitan Transport Authority appointed* on the 20th February, 2015, for a period of one (1) year, with effect from the 1st March, 2016
Dated the 4th March, 2016.
JAMES MACHARIA,
Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure.
*G.N. 1093/2015.
