THE CIVIL PROCEDURE ACT
(CAP.  21)
ESTABLISHMENT
TAKE NOTICE that the Chief Justice/President, Supreme Court of Kenya has established Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) Law Courts, a magistrate’s court that is within the supervisory jurisdiction of Nairobi High Court, with effect from the 16th May, 2016.
Dated the 5th May, 2016.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice and President, Supreme Court of Kenya.
