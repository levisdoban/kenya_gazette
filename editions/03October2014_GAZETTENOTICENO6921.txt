REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Thura Mutuambae (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Abogeta/U-kithangari/219, situate in the district of Meru, and whereas the High Court in Succession Cause No. 96 of 2013, has issued grant of letters of administration 1/219 and certificate of confirmation of grant in favour of M'ltonga M'Thura (ID/2523707), and whereas the land title deed in respect of Thura Mutuambae (deceased) to the said piece of land registered in the name is lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said application to be registered as proprietor by transmission R.L. 19 in the name of M'ltonga M'Thura (ID/2523707) and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Thura Mutuambae (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
