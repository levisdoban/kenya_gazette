THE ENERGY ACT
(NO. 12 OF 2006)
APPLICATION FOR ADJUSTMENT OF ROAD MAINTENANCE LEVY
PURSUANT to the provisions of section 5, 6 and 25 of the Energy Act, 2006 and provisions of the Energy (Petroleum Pricing) Regulations of 2010, the Energy Regulatory Commission notifies the general public that it has considered an application from Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) for the recovery of levies under the Road Maintenance Levy Fund (Imposition of Levy) (Amendment) Order, 2015 and made a decision as follows:
(a) Petroleum pricing as stipulated in the pricing regulations is based on volumes of cargoes discharged from the 10th of the previous month to the 9th of the pricing month.
(b) Cargoes that were discharged from 17th July, 2015 to 9th August 2015 were fully compensated during the pricing period of 15th August, 2015 to 14th September, 2015. Accordingly the request for additional compensation is disallowed.
The decisions are based on the objects and mandate of the Commission as provided under sections 5 (b) and 6 (d) of the Energy Act.
 Dated the 19th April, 2016.
JOSEPH NG’ANG’A,
MR/PTG/1508/15-16                                         Director-General.
