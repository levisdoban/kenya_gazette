IN THE HIGH COURT OF KENYA AT BUSIA
PROBATE AND ADMINISTRATION
TAKE NOTICE that applications having been made in this court in:
Cause No. 409 of 2015
By Constant Onyango Barasa, of P.O. Box 575, Busia in Kenya, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Joseph Barasa Owuor, who died on 12th April, 2015.
Cause No. 461 of 2015
By Joseph Maloba Namatsi, of P.O. Box 92, Buhuyi, for a grant of letters of administration intestate to the estate of Peter Namatsi Oola, who died at Elukongo Sub-location, on 5th May, 1984.
The Court will proceed to issue the same unless cause be shown to the contrary and appearance in this respect entered within thirty (30) days from the date of publication of this notice in the Kenya Gazette.
Dated the 9th December, 2015.
M. A. NANZUSHI,
                                                    Deputy Registrar, Busia (K).
