THE HIGH COURT (ORGANIZATION AND ADMINISTRATION) ACT
(No. 27 of 2015)
NOMINATION
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 23 (2) (b) of the High Court (Organization and Administration) Act, the Principal Judge of the High Court nominates—
LADY JUSTICE CHRISTINE W. MEOLI
Presiding Judge of the Naivasha High Court, as a member of the High Court Advisory Committee, for a period of three (3) years with effect from 15th February, 2016.
Dated the 10th March, 2016.
RICHARD MWONGO,
Principal Judge of the High Court of Kenya.
