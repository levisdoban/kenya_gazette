NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME
IT IS notified for general information that the specified bank formerly known as Oriental Commercial Bank Limited has by a resolution of the Board and the approval of the Registrar of Companies, effected a change of name from Oriental Commercial Bank Limited to M-Oriental Bank Limited with effect from the 6th June, 2016.

PATRICK NJOROGE,
Governor, Central Bank of Kenya.
