THE SUPREME COURT OF KENYA
Christmas Vacation, 2014
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by Article 161 (2) (a) of  the Constitution of Kenya, Rule 4 (c) of the Supreme Court Rules 2012, the Chief Justice gives notice as follows:
The Supreme Court Christmas Vacation shall commence on 22nd December, 2014, and terminate on 13th January, 2015 both days inclusive.
A Judge will hear urgent matters, if admitted to hearing on application made in accordance with the Rules of the Court. Vacation Duty Judges shall be appointed by the President of the Court in accordance with the Rules of the Court.
During the period the Supreme Court registry shall be open to the public from 8.00 a.m. to 12 noon on all weekdays other than public holidays.
Dated the 8th December, 2012.
WILLY MUTUNGA,
Chief Justice/President,
            Supreme Court of Kenya.
