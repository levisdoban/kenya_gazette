THE LABOUR RELATIONS ACT
(No. 14 of 2007)
Application for Registration of Trade Union
NOTICE is given pursuant to section 14, 15 and 17 of the Labour Relations Act, to all trade unions, federation of trade unions, employers organizations or federation, of the receipt of application for registration of the following:
National Union of Shops-Retailers and General Merchants Workers
The notice is given to the Kenya Union of Commercial Food and Allied Workers, to submit in writing any such objections against the applicants within twenty-one (21) days from the date of publication of this notice.
E. N. GICHEHA,
MR/8055247                  Registrar of Trade Unions.
