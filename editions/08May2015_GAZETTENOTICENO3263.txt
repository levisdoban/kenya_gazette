THE LAND ACT

CONSTRUCTION OF MASARA–SUNA–KEHANCHA (C13) ROAD AND REINSTATEMENT OF AWENDO—MARIWA (D202) ROAD PROJECT
Intention to Acquire Land
Addendum 
IN PURSUANCE of transitional provisions contained in section 162 (2) of the Land Act, 2012 and section 9 (1) of the Land Acquisition Act (Cap. 295) (repealed), and further to Gazette Notice Nos. 2728 and 2925 of 2014, the National Land Commission intend to acquire additional land parcels given below on behalf of Kenya National Highways Authority for the construction of Masara–Suna–Kehancha (C13) Road and Reinstatement of Awendo–Mariwa (D202) Road Project in the Counties of Migori and Suba. Inquiries for hearing of claims to compensation by people interested in the additional land to be acquired for the construction of Masara–Suna–Kehancha (C13) Road and Reinstatement of Awendo–Mariwa (D202) Road Project shall be held on the dates and places shown here below.
