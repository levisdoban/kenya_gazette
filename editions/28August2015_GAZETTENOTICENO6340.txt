THE COMPETITION ACT
(No. 12 of 2010)
PROPOSED ACQUISITION
PURSUANT to section 46 (6) (a) (ii) of the Competition Act, 2010, it is notified for general information that the Competition Authority of Kenya has authorized the proposed acquisition of 51 per cent of the issued share capital of Transmara Sugar Limited by Sucriere Des Mascareignes Limited.
Dated the 29th July, 2015.
WANG’OMBE KARIUKI,
Director-General.
