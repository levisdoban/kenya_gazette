THE KENYA POST OFFICE SAVINGS BANK ACT
(Cap. 493B)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 5 (2) (e) of the Kenya Post Office Savings Bank Act, the Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury appoints—
Lawrence Tonui,
Jane Moronge,
to be members of the Board of  Kenya Post Office Savings Bank, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 2nd October, 2015 and revokes the appointment of Edward M. Ngigi*.
Dated the 2nd October, 2015.
HENRY ROTICH,
Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury. 
*G.N. 3450/2015.
