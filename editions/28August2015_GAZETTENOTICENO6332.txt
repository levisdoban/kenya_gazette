THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Titus Gitau Muriu (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land containing 4.7 acres or thereabout, known as Karai/Gikambura/565, situate in the district of Kiambu, and whereas the High Court at Nairobi in succession cause No. 2119 of 2009, has issued grant of letters of administration to Lee Ngugi Gitau, and whereas the said title deed issued to Titus Gitau Muriu (deceased) has been reported missing or lost, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said instrument of R. L. 19 and R. L. 7, and issue a land title deed to the said Lee Ngugi Gitau, and upon such registration the land title deed issued to Titus Gitau Muriu (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 28th August, 2015.
I. N. NJIRU,
MR/8055141                               Land Registrar, Kiambu District.
