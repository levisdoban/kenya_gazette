THE NATIONAL MUSEUMS AND HERITAGE ACT
(No. 6 of 2006)
Declaration of Monument
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 25 (1) (c) of the National Museums and Heritage Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Sports, Culture and the Arts, declares the site specified in the Schedule, which he considers to be of pre-historical interest, to be a National Monument within the meaning of the Act.
Any objection to the declaration shall be lodged with the Cabinet Secretary within sixty (60) days from the date of publication of this notice.
Schedule
NARIOKOTOME SITE
All that area of land known as Nariokotome Site also known as the Turkana Boy Site, measuring approximately 45 metres by 60 metres on geographical co-ordinates 35° 52 38.78” E, 4° 7 52.54” N, situated in Lowangerak Sub-location, Lokitaung Division of Turkana County.
Dated the 19th December, 2014.
HASSAN WARIO ARERO,
Cabinet Secretary, Ministry of Sports, Culture and the Arts.
