Starting from a point A, which is an IPC from which spotheight 231 and spot height 203 are 2.3 kilometres and 1.4 kilometres distant on bearing of 185° 30' 00" and 262° 30' 00" respectively; thence for a distance of 125.72 metres on a bearing of 191° 56' 08" to point B; thence for a distance of 83.70 metres on a bearing of 323° 44' 46" to point C an IPC; thence for a distance of 93.30 metres on a bearing of 53° 29' 55" to starting point.
