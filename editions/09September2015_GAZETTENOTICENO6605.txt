
				Page: 2067GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 6605
THE KENYA DEFENCE FORCES ACT
(No. 25 of 2012)
DEPLOYMENT
PURSUANT to Article 241 (3) (b) of the Constitution, as read together with sections 31 (a), 33 (1), 34 (2) and 34 (4) (a) of the Kenya Defence Forces Act, 2012, notice is given that on the 8th September, 2015, the Kenya Defence Forces were deployed in support of the National Police Service in Boni Forest which traverses the counties of Lamu, Tana River and Garissa in response to the security emergency existing in the aforestated area.
Dated the 8th September, 2015.
RAYCHELLE OMAMO,
Cabinet Secretary for Defence.
