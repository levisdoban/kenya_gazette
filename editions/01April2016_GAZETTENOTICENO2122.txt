THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT
(No. 3 of 2012)
DEREGISTRATION OF A PROVISIONAL CERTIFICATE OF TITLE
WHEREAS the City Council of Nairobi issued a lease to Red Kaka Limited on all that piece of land known as L.R. No. 11927/3, situate in the city of Nairobi in the Nairobi Area, registered as I.R. 111940/1, and whereas a transfer was duly registered on 19th August, 2008 in favour of Mohamud Mohamed Mohamud, provisional certificate of title was issued vide GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 7758 of
31st October, 2014 in favour of Red Kaka Limited, and whereas the said Mohamed Mohamud Mohamed has produced the original lease I.R. No. 111940/1 before the registrar of titles for verification, the registrar of titles hereby gives notice that the provisional certificate of title issued to Red Kaka Limited on 5th March, 2015, stands deregistered and cancelled.
Dated the 1st April, 2016.
