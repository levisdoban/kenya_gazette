THE BETTING, LOTTERIES AND GAMING ACT
(Cap. 131)
APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3 (1) (a) of the Betting, Lotteries and Gaming Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and Co-ordination of National Government appoints—
Anthony Kimani Kung’u
as the Chairman of the Betting Control and Licensing Board, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from 25th January, 2016.
Dated the 22nd January, 2016.
JOSEPH NKAISSERY,
Cabinet Secretary,
Ministry of Interior and Co-ordination of National Government.
