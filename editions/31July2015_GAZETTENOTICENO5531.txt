THE KENYA INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS ACT
(Cap. 411A)
TASKFORCE TO REVIEW THE KENYA INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION (BROADCASTING) REGULATIONS, 2010
EXTENSION OF TERM
PURSUANT to the appointment of the Taskforce to review the Information and Communication (Broadcasting) Regulations, 2010, vide GAZETTE NOTICE NO.  3136 of the 6th May, 2015, the Cabinet Secretary for Information, Communications and Technology extends the period for the completion of the work of the Taskforce to the 6th September, 2015.
Dated the 13th July, 2015.
FRED MATIANG’I,
Cabinet Secretary for
Information, Communications and Technology.
