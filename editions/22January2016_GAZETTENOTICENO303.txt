THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS (1) Pauline Wangari Thuo (ID/19099115) and (2) John Gathu Mbote (ID/4288772), are registered as proprietors of that piece of land containing 0.9850 hectare or thereabouts, known as Dundori/Lanet Block 5/303 (Kiamunyeki ‘A’), situate in the district of Nakuru, and whereas the High Court at Nakuru in civil suit No. 33 of 1998 has ordered that the said piece of land be transferred to Baragu Daniel (ID/5496221), and whereas all efforts made to compel (1) Pauline Wangari Thuo (ID/19099115) and (2) John Gathu Mbote (ID/4288772), to surrender the said land title deed in respect of the said piece of land, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with the registration of the said court order and issue a new title deed to the said Baragu Daniel (ID/5496221), and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said (1) Pauline Wangari Thuo (ID/19099115) and (2) John Gathu Mbote (ID/4288772), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 22nd January, 2016.
