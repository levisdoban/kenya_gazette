THE LAND ACT

REHABILITATION OF KISUMU—KAKAMEGA (Al) ROAD, KAKAMEGA—WEBUYE (Al) ROAD AND WEBUYE—KITALE (Al) ROAD
INTENTION TO ACQUIRE LAND/INQUIRY
Addendum to Gazette Notice Nos. 8132 and 7699 of 2014.
IN PURSUANCE of section 162 (2) of the Land Act, 2012 and further to Gazette Notice Nos. 8132 and 7699 of 2014, the National Land Commission intends to acquire the plots given below on behalf of Kenya National Highways Authority (KENHA) for the rehabilitation of the Kisumu–Kakamega (A1) Road and Webuye-Kitale (Al) Road in the counties of Vihiga, Kakamega, Bungoma, Kisumu and Nandi. Inquiries for hearing of claims to compensation by people interested in the land to be acquired for the road construction shall be held on the dates and places shown here below.
