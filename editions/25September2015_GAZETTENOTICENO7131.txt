REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS John Willington Oyomba (deceased), of Yala in the Republic of Kenya, is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as South Gem/Rera/1446, situate in the district of Siaya, and whereas the High Court at Kisumu in succession cause No. H.C./R.
