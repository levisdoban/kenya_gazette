APPOINTMENT
PURSUANT to section 15 of the Sixth Schedule to the Constitution of Kenya as read with section 23 and 24 of the Transition to Devolved Governments Act, 2012, and further to Legal Notice No. 16 of the 2013, through which the Transition Authority approved the transfer of functions specified in the Schedule of the County Government of Kiambu, with effect from 9th August, 2013, vide Kenya Gazette Supplement No. 116 of 2013, the County Executive Committee Member for Health in Kiambu County do appoint the underlisted as members of the Kiambu County Level 3 and Level 2 health facilities (health centres and dispensaries) management committees as indicated in respective hospitals and health facilities, for a period of three (3) years.
