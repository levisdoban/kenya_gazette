THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT

REGISTRATION OF INSTRUMENT
WHEREAS Fulgence Mwamachi Mganga (deceased), is registered as proprietor of that piece of land known as Bura/Mrugua/945, situate in the district of Taita/Taveta, and whereas the High Court at Nairobi in succession cause No. 2539 of 2003, has issued grant of letters of administration to (1) Theresian Kijala Mwamachi and (2) Pantaleo Ngura Mwamachi, and whereas all efforts made to recover the land title deed and surrender to the land registrar for cancellation have failed, notice is given that after the expiration of thirty (30) days from the date hereof, provided no valid objection has been received within that period, I intend to dispense with the production of the said land title deed and proceed with registration of the said administration letters to the said (1) Theresian Kijala Mwamachi and (2) Pantaleo Ngura Mwamachi, and upon such registration the land title deed issued earlier to the said Fulgence Mwamachi Mganga (deceased), shall be deemed to be cancelled and of no effect.
Dated the 30th October, 2015.
