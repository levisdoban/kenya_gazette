THE COMPETITION ACT
(No. 12 of 2010)
NOTICE OF SETTLEMENT
IT IS notified for general information pursuant to section 39 of the Competition Act, 2010, that in exercise of the powers conferred by section 38 of the Act, the Competition Authority of Kenya has entered into a settlement agreement with the Association of Kenya Reinsurers after the Association was found to have contravened section 21 (3) (a) of the Act.
Under the settlement agreement—
(a) the Authority imposed a fine of seven hundred and twenty-one thousand, seven hundred and fifteen shillings on the Association; and
(b) the Association gave a written undertaking to in future desist from engaging in any conduct which is in contravention of the Act.
Dated the 4th May, 2015.
WANG’OMBE KARIUKI,
Director-General.
