THE COUNTY GOVERNMENTS ACT
(No. 17 of 2012)
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF BOMET
PURSUANT to Standing Order 29 of the Standing Orders of the County Assembly of Bomet, it is notified for the information of Members of the County Assembly of Bomet and the general public that the assembly shall have a special sitting on Monday, 14th of September, 2015, at 10.00 a.m. at the County Assembly Chamber, in Bomet, for the first Reading of the Bomet County Access to Information Bill, 2015 (Bills No. 8), and to discuss the reason as to why the County Assembly Work Plan was varied by the Executive when requesting money from the Controller of Budget and the National Treasury without justified reasons and this has hampered the operations of the County Assembly.
Dated the 10th of September, 2015.
G. K. KIPNGETICH,
Speaker.
