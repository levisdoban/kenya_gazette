THE KENYA RAILWAYS CORPORATION ACT {Cap. 397) APPOINTMENT
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 4 (a) of the Kenya Railways Corporation Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure appoints —
Gen. (Rtd.) Jeremiah Mutinda Kianga
to be the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Kenya Railways Corporation, for a period of three (3) years, with effect from the 20th July, 2014.
Dated the 30th September, 2014.
M. S. M. KAMAU.Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure.
