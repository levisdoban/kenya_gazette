GAZETTE NOTICE NO. 4006
THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA
TRIBUNAL TO INVESTIGATE THE CONDUCT OF MUMO MATEMU, MEMBER AND CHAIRPERSON OF THE ETHICS AND ANTI-CORRUPTION COMMISSION AND IRENE KEINO (MS.), MEMBER AND VICE-CHAIRPERSON OF THE ETHICS AND ANTI-CORRUPTION COMMISSION
REVOCATION OF APPOINTMENT
WHEREAS by Gazette Notice No. 2822 of 2015, I appointed a Tribunal in exercise of the powers conferred on me by Article 251 (4) (b) of the Constitution, to investigate the conduct of Mumo Matemu, Member and Chairperson of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission and Irene Keino (Ms.), Member and Vice-Chairperson of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission and determine whether the two should be removed from office;
AND WHEREAS subsequent to that appointment, Mumo Matemu, Member and Chairperson of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission and Irene Keino (Ms.), Member and Vice-Chairperson of the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission, resigned from holding office as such on the 12th May, 2015 and the 30th April, 2015, respectively, thereby rendering the Tribunal no longer necessary.
NOW THEREFORE, I, Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Kenya Defence Forces, revoke Gazette Notice No. 2822 of 2015 and dissolve the Tribunal accordingly, with effect from the 15th May, 2015.
Dated the 28th May, 2015.
UHURU KENYATTA,
President.
